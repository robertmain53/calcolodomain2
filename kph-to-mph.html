Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on standard conversion formulas. For reliable speed conversions, we adhere to the conversion coefficient where 1 kilometer per hour is equivalent to approximately 0.621371 miles per hour.

The Formula Explained

To convert kilometers per hour to miles per hour, use the formula:

\( \text{mph} = \text{kph} \times 0.621371 \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Kilometers per Hour (kph): The number of kilometers traveled in one hour.
  • Miles per Hour (mph): The number of miles traveled in one hour.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, to convert 100 kph to mph, multiply 100 by 0.621371 to get approximately 62.1371 mph.
