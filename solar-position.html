The Formula Explained

Solar Azimuth: \( A = \arctan\left(\frac{-\sin(h)}{\tan(\delta) \cdot \cos(\phi) - \sin(\phi) \cdot \cos(h)}\right) \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Latitude: The geographic coordinate specifying the north-south position of a point on Earth's surface.
  • Longitude: The geographic coordinate specifying the east-west position of a point on Earth's surface.
  • Solar Elevation: The apparent angle of the sun above the horizon.
  • Solar Azimuth: The compass direction from which the sunlight is coming at any specific point on the earth's surface.
