Data Source and Methodology

All calculations use the standard U.S. academic grading scale. Please refer to your institution for any specific variations.

The Formula Explained

\[ \text{GPA} = \frac{\sum (\text{Grade Points} \times \text{Credits})}{\text{Total Credits}} \]

Glossary of Terms

  • GPA: Grade Point Average.
  • Grade Points: Numerical equivalent of letter grades.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a GPA?

GPA stands for Grade Point Average, a standard way of measuring academic achievement in the U.S.

How do I calculate my GPA?

Add up all your grades, divide by the number of classes, and convert to a scale of 4.0.
