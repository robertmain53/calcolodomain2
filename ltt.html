Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Land Transaction Tax?

LTT is a tax on property and land transactions in Wales, replacing Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) in 2018.

Who needs to pay LTT?

Anyone purchasing a property or land in Wales above a certain price threshold is required to pay LTT.

How is LTT calculated?

LTT is calculated based on the value of the property and the type of property being purchased.

Are there any exemptions?

Yes, certain transactions such as transfers between spouses are exempt. Always check the latest guidelines.

Where can I find more information?

Visit the official Welsh Government website for the latest LTT rules and rates.
