Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on simple arithmetic operations, ensuring accuracy and reliability. Please follow standardized grading criteria as per your educational institution.

The Formula Explained

Score Percentage: Score = (Correct Answers / Total Questions) × 100

Glossary of Terms

  • Total Questions: The total number of questions in the test.
  • Correct Answers: The number of questions answered correctly.
  • Score Percentage: The calculated score expressed as a percentage.

Practical Example

For instance, if you answered 18 out of 20 questions correctly, your score would be calculated as follows:

Score = (18 / 20) × 100 = 90%

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can this calculator handle fractional scores?

Our calculator is designed for integer values of correct answers and total questions. For fractional scoring systems, please adjust your inputs accordingly.

What should I do if my score is lower than expected?

Review your answers and ensure all correct responses were counted. You may also consult your instructor for further clarification.
