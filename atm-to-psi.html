The Formula Explained

\[ \text{PSI} = \text{Atmospheres} \times 14.696 \]

Glossary of Terms

  • Atmospheres (atm): A unit of pressure based on the average atmospheric pressure at sea level.
  • PSI: Pounds per square inch, a unit of pressure.
