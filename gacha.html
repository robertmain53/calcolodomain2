Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a gacha game?

Gacha games are games that use a loot box mechanic, where players can spend currency to receive a random item.

How is the probability calculated?

The probability is calculated using the formula for independent events, helping predict the chance of obtaining specific items.

Can I improve my chances?

Yes, by increasing the number of draws or focusing on events with higher drop rates.

Do all items have the same probability?

Not necessarily. Some items may have higher or lower probabilities based on the game's design.

Why use this calculator?
