What is PSI?

PSI stands for pounds per square inch, a unit of pressure commonly used in various fields, including engineering and meteorology.

How do I convert PSI to Pascals?

Multiply the PSI value by 6894.76 to convert it to Pascals.

Why use Pascals?

Pascals are the SI unit of pressure and are widely used in scientific contexts.
