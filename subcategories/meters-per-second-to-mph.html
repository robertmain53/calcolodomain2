Formula

mph = m/s × 2.23694

To convert, multiply your value in meters per second by 2.23694. This factor is based on 1 mile = 1609.344 meters and 1 hour = 3600 seconds.

Example Calculation

  • 12 m/s × 2.23694 = 26.84 mph

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert m/s to mph?

Multiply by 2.23694. Example: 15 m/s × 2.23694 = 33.55 mph.

Which is faster, 1 m/s or 1 mph?

1 m/s ≈ 2.24 mph. So 1 m/s is faster!
