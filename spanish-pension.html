The Formula Explained

\( \text{Pension} = \frac{\text{Total Contributions} \times \text{Average Contribution Rate}}{\text{Life Expectancy} - \text{Retirement Age}} \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Current Age: Your current age.
  • Expected Retirement Age: The age at which you plan to retire.
  • Monthly Contribution: The amount you contribute monthly to your pension plan.
  • Estimated Monthly Pension: The estimated pension you will receive monthly after retirement.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How accurate is this calculator?

This calculator provides estimates based on current contribution rates and government data. Actual results may vary.

Can I change my retirement age?

Yes, you can adjust your expected retirement age in the input fields to see how it affects your pension.
