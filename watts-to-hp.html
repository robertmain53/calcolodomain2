The Formula Explained

Horsepower (HP) = Watts / 745.7

Glossary of Terms

  • Watts: The SI unit of power, equivalent to one joule per second.
  • Horsepower: A unit of power in the British system of units equal to 745.7 watts.

What is the conversion factor from watts to horsepower?

1 horsepower is equivalent to 745.7 watts.

How do I convert watts to horsepower?

To convert watts to horsepower, divide the power value in watts by 745.7.

Why is horsepower used?

Horsepower is a unit of measurement for power commonly used in the automotive and engineering sectors to describe the power output of engines.
