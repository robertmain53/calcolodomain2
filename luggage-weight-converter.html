Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on standard conversion formulas. Conversion rates: 1 kg = 2.20462 lbs.

The Formula Explained

To convert from kilograms to pounds: \( \text{Pounds} = \text{Kilograms} \times 2.20462 \)

To convert from pounds to kilograms: \( \text{Kilograms} = \text{Pounds} \div 2.20462 \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Kilograms (kg): A unit of mass in the metric system.
  • Pounds (lb): A unit of weight in the imperial system.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

Example: Convert 10 kg to pounds. Using the formula: \( \text{Pounds} = 10 \times 2.20462 = 22.0462 \) pounds.
