Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a rem?

A rem is a unit of effective radiation dose, used to measure the risk of radiation exposure to human health.

Why convert rems to sieverts?

Sieverts are the SI unit for radiation dose, providing a standard for comparing radiation exposure across different contexts.
