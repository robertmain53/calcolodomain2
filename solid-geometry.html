The Formula Explained

Sphere Volume: \( V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3 \)

Sphere Surface Area: \( SA = 4 \pi r^2 \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Volume: The amount of space occupied by the shape.
  • Surface Area: The total area of the surface of a three-dimensional object.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

To calculate the volume of a sphere with a radius of 5:

\( V = \frac{4}{3} \pi (5^3) = 523.6 \) cubic units.
