The Formula Explained

Conversion Formula: Cups = Grams / 236.588

Glossary of Variables

  • Grams: The weight of the ingredient in grams.
  • Cups: The equivalent volume in cups.

Practical Example

For instance, to convert 100 grams of flour to cups: 100 / 236.588 = 0.42 cups.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the conversion rate for granulated sugar?

For granulated sugar, 1 cup is approximately 200 grams.

Why do different ingredients have different conversion rates?

The conversion depends on the density of the ingredient, which affects how much volume is occupied by a certain weight.

How can I ensure accurate conversions?

Using a kitchen scale is the most accurate method for converting grams to cups.
