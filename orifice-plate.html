The Formula Explained

Flow Rate Formula: Q = C × A × sqrt((2 × ΔP) / ρ)

Glossary of Terms

  • Q: Flow Rate (L/s)
  • C: Discharge Coefficient
  • A: Orifice Area (m²)
  • ΔP: Pressure Drop (Pa)
  • ρ: Fluid Density (kg/m³)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is an orifice plate?

An orifice plate is a device used for measuring flow rate, reducing pressure, or restricting flow in piping systems.

How do I choose the correct orifice diameter?

The diameter of the orifice must be selected based on the specific application requirements, including flow rate and pressure drop considerations.

What factors affect flow measurement accuracy?

Factors such as fluid density, viscosity, temperature, and pressure must be considered for accurate flow measurement.
