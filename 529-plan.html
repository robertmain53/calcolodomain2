Glossary of Terms

  • Current Age: The current age of the child.
  • Age When College Starts: The age at which the child will begin college.
  • Current Savings Amount: The amount already saved in the 529 Plan.
  • Monthly Contribution: The monthly amount you plan to add to the savings.
  • Expected Annual Return (%): The annual growth rate expected from the savings.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a 529 Plan?

A 529 Plan is a tax-advantaged savings plan designed to encourage saving for future education costs.

How do I determine the amount to save?

Estimate future college costs and use tools like our 529 Plan Calculator to determine how much you need to save monthly.

Are there any tax benefits with a 529 Plan?

Yes, earnings in a 529 Plan grow federal tax-free and are not taxed when used for qualified education expenses.
