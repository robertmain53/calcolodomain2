Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Smith Chart?

A Smith Chart is a graphical aid used in electrical engineering to solve problems involving transmission lines and matching circuits.

How do you use a Smith Chart for impedance matching?

Smith Charts are used to plot complex impedances and can help visualize and solve impedance matching problems by using reflection coefficients and other parameters.
