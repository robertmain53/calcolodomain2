Formula

mph = ft/s × 0.681818

1 foot per second is exactly 0.681818 mph.
To convert, multiply feet per second by this constant.

How Does the Conversion Work?

There are 5280 feet in a mile and 3600 seconds in an hour.
So, 1 ft/s × (3600 s/hr) ÷ 5280 ft/mi = 0.681818 mph.

Example Calculation

  1. Convert 45 ft/s to mph
  2. 45 × 0.681818 = 30.68 mph

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert feet per second to miles per hour?

Multiply the speed in ft/s by 0.681818. Example: 50 ft/s × 0.681818 = 34.09 mph.

Is 1 ft/s faster than 1 mph?

No, 1 ft/s is about 0.68 mph. 1 mph = 1.467 ft/s.

When is this conversion useful?

Whenever you measure speed in US customary units and want mph for comparison (or vice versa), e.g., for safety evaluations, speed records, ballistics, and scientific work.
