The Formula Explained

Cost Per Hire = \(\frac{\text{Total Recruitment Costs}}{\text{Number of Hires}}\)

Glossary of Terms

  • Total Recruitment Costs: The sum of all expenses associated with recruiting new employees.
  • Number of Hires: The total number of employees hired during the period.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What expenses are included in total recruitment costs?

It includes advertising, agency fees, recruitment software, and staff time spent on recruitment.

Why is calculating cost per hire important?

It helps organizations evaluate the efficiency of their recruitment processes and optimize spending.
