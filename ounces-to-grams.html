Data Source and Methodology

The conversion is based on the standard formula where 1 ounce is equivalent to 28.3495 grams. All calculations adhere strictly to this conversion factor.

The Formula Explained

\[ \text{Grams} = \text{Ounces} \times 28.3495 \]

Glossary of Variables

  • Ounces: The weight in ounces you wish to convert.
  • Grams: The equivalent weight in grams.

Example Calculation

To convert 5 ounces to grams: \[ 5 \times 28.3495 = 141.7475 \] Therefore, 5 ounces is approximately 141.75 grams.
