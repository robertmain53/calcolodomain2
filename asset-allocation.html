  • Bonds: Debt investments offering fixed returns.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is asset allocation?

Asset allocation involves dividing an investment portfolio among different asset categories, such as stocks, bonds, and cash.

Why is asset allocation important?

It helps to balance risk and reward by diversifying investments.

How do I determine my risk tolerance?

Your risk tolerance depends on your financial situation, investment goals, and comfort with potential losses.
