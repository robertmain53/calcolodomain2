Final Grade Calculator

This calculator helps students determine the score needed on their final exam to achieve their desired overall grade in a course. It's perfect for students preparing for finals who need to understand what score will allow them to reach their academic goals.

Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on standard educational grading formulas. For more information, consult your educational institution's guidelines.

The Formula Explained

\( \text{Score Needed} = \frac{(\text{Desired Grade} \times 100) - (\text{Current Grade} \times (100 - \text{Final Exam Weight}))}{\text{Final Exam Weight}} \)

Glossary of Variables

  • Current Grade: Your current grade in the course.
  • Desired Grade: The overall grade you wish to achieve.
  • Final Exam Weight: The percentage of your final grade that is determined by your final exam score.
  • Score Needed: The score you need to achieve on the final exam to reach your desired grade.
