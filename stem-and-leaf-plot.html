The Formula Explained

Given a dataset, each number is split into a 'stem' and a 'leaf'. The 'stem' is typically the leading digit(s), and the 'leaf' is the trailing digit(s).

Glossary of Terms

  • Stem: The leading digit(s) in a number.
  • Leaf: The trailing digit(s) in a number.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a stem and leaf plot?

A stem and leaf plot is a statistical tool used to display quantitative data in a graphical format, where each data point is split into a 'stem' and a 'leaf'.

How do I enter data?

Enter your data as a comma-separated list of numbers in the input field provided.

Can I use this for large datasets?

Yes, but ensure your data is formatted correctly and is within a reasonable size for quick processing.
