The Formula Explained

Area = b × h

Glossary of Variables

  • Base (b): The length of the base of the parallelogram.
  • Height (h): The perpendicular height from the base to the opposite side.

Practical Example

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, if the base of a parallelogram is 5 units and the height is 3 units, the area will be calculated as 5 × 3 = 15 square units.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a parallelogram?

A parallelogram is a four-sided shape where opposite sides are parallel.

What units are used for area?

The area is typically measured in square units, such as square meters or square inches.
