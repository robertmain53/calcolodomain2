How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

To convert 100 mph to kph: Multiply 100 by 1.60934 (conversion factor) to get 160.934 kph.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a knot in speed measurement?

A knot is a measure of speed equivalent to one nautical mile per hour, commonly used in maritime and aviation contexts.

How do I convert from mph to kph?

To convert mph to kph, multiply the speed by 1.60934.

Is this calculator accurate?

Yes, our calculator uses precise conversion factors from authoritative sources.

Why use meters per second?

Meters per second is the SI unit for speed and is used in scientific contexts.

Can I use this for aviation?

Yes, this converter supports knots, which is commonly used in aviation.
