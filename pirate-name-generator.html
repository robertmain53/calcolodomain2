Our Pirate Name Generator is based on a fun algorithm that combines parts of your real name with common pirate monikers. All calculations are for entertainment purposes only.

The Formula Explained

Your Pirate Name is generated by combining your first initial with a common pirate first name and your last initial with a pirate last name.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, if your name is John Smith, your pirate name might be Captain Jack Sparrow. We take the 'J' from John and match it with a common pirate name starting with 'J', and 'S' from Smith for the last name.
