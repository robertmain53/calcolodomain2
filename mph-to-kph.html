Formula

\[ \text{km/h} = \text{mph} \times 1.60934 \]

Where 1 mile per hour equals approximately 1.60934 kilometers per hour.

How It Works

Miles per hour (mph) and kilometers per hour (kph or km/h) are both units of speed. The conversion factor 1.60934 comes from the relationship between miles and kilometers: 1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers. This converter is essential when traveling between countries that use different measurement systems or when reading speedometers calibrated for different markets.

Example Calculation

To convert 60 mph to km/h:

  1. Multiply 60 mph by the conversion factor 1.60934
  2. 60 × 1.60934 = 96.56 km/h
  3. Result: 60 mph = 96.56 km/h

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert mph to kph?

To convert mph to kph, multiply the speed in miles per hour by 1.60934. For example: 50 mph × 1.60934 = 80.47 km/h.

What is the difference between mph and kph?

MPH (miles per hour) is used primarily in the United States and UK, while KPH (kilometers per hour) is used in most other countries worldwide. 1 mph equals approximately 1.609 km/h.

Is 100 kph faster than 60 mph?

Yes. 100 km/h equals approximately 62.14 mph, which is slightly faster than 60 mph.

Why is the conversion factor 1.60934?

The conversion factor comes from the exact relationship: 1 mile = 1.609344 kilometers. We typically round this to 1.60934 for practical calculations.

Data Source and Methodology

The conversion factor is based on the international standard: 1 mile = 1.609344 kilometers exactly. This is defined by international agreement and is used worldwide for accurate speed conversions.
