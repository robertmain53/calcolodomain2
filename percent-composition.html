How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

To calculate the percent composition of H2O:

  1. Determine the molar mass of each element.
  2. Calculate the total mass of the compound.
  3. Divide the mass of each element by the total mass and multiply by 100.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is percent composition?

Percent composition refers to the percentage of each element within a compound, calculated by mass.

How do I calculate percent composition?

Calculate the molar mass of each element, find the total mass of the compound, and divide the mass of each element by the total mass. Multiply by 100 for the percent.

Why is percent composition important?

It helps in understanding the proportion of elements in a compound, essential for chemical analysis.
