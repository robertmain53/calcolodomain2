The Formula Explained

Total Budget = Accommodation + Transportation + Food + Entertainment + Miscellaneous

Glossary of Terms

  • Accommodation: Costs related to lodging during your trip.
  • Transportation: Expenses for travel, including flights, car rentals, taxis, etc.
  • Food: Costs for meals and beverages.
  • Entertainment: Expenses for activities and attractions.
  • Miscellaneous: Other expenses such as souvenirs, tips, etc.
