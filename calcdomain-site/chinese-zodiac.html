The Formula Explained

Zodiac Sign = (Year - 4) % 12

Glossary of Terms

  • Birth Year: The year you were born in the Gregorian calendar.
  • Zodiac Sign: One of the 12 animals of the Chinese Zodiac.
  • Characteristics: Traits associated with each zodiac sign.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, if you were born in 1990, calculate (1990 - 4) % 12 to find your sign. The result is 6, corresponding to the Horse in the Chinese Zodiac.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Chinese Zodiac?

The Chinese Zodiac is a classification scheme based on the lunar calendar that assigns an animal to each year in a 12-year cycle.

How accurate is the Chinese Zodiac?

While the Chinese Zodiac is a cultural tradition, it can provide insights into personality traits and compatibility with others.

How can I use my Chinese Zodiac sign?

Many people use their Zodiac signs to understand their own behaviors and relationships with others.

Are there any exceptions or special cases?

The Chinese Zodiac traditionally begins on the Chinese New Year, so those born in January or February should check when the Lunar New Year started that year.

Can I find compatibility with other signs?

Yes, the Chinese Zodiac is often used to determine compatibility between signs, especially in relationships.
