Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Vincenty's Formulae?

Vincenty's formulae are used to calculate the distance between two points on the surface of a spheroid.

How accurate is this calculator?

The calculator provides highly accurate distances by using ellipsoidal models of the Earth.

Can this calculator be used for air navigation?

Yes, due to its precision, it is suitable for air and marine navigation.

How do I convert geographic coordinates to decimal?

Use the formula: Degrees + (Minutes/60) + (Seconds/3600).

Why are ellipsoids used instead of spheres?

Ellipsoids provide a more accurate representation of Earth, accounting for polar flattening.
