Glossary of Terms

  • Password Length: The number of characters in the password.
  • Uppercase Letters: Capital letters (A-Z) that can be included in the password.
  • Numbers: Digits (0-9) that can be included in the password.
  • Symbols: Special characters that can increase the complexity of the password.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why use a password generator?

Using a password generator creates strong and unique passwords, reducing the risk of security breaches.

Are generated passwords secure?

Yes, generated passwords are designed to be random and complex, making them difficult for attackers to guess.

How long should my password be?

A password should be at least 12 characters long, but longer passwords provide more security.

Should I include symbols in my password?

Including symbols in your password increases its complexity and security.

Can I customize the character set?

Yes, you can choose to include or exclude uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
