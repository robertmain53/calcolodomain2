Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

CalcDomain is built privacy-first. Most calculators run locally in your browser; we avoid collecting personal data and only use minimal, anonymous analytics to improve the service.

1) Information We Collect

  • No account required. You can use CalcDomain without registering or sharing personal information.
  • Calculator inputs stay local. We design calculators to compute in your browser. Input values are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Anonymous usage analytics (optional/limited). We may measure general usage (e.g., page views, device type, approximate region) in aggregate. We do not build user profiles or attempt to identify you.
  • Basic technical logs. Our servers may automatically record standard information (e.g., IP address, user agent, date/time, requested resource) for security and reliability.
We do not knowingly collect sensitive categories of data. Please avoid entering confidential or personal information into free-text fields.

2) How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our calculators and website
  • Monitor availability, performance, and abuse prevention
  • Understand which tools are most helpful (in aggregate)
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms

Legal bases (EEA/UK): performance of a contract (providing the service you request), legitimate interests (service improvement, security), and consent where required for any non-essential cookies/analytics.

3) Cookies & Local Storage

  • Essential cookies. Used for core functionality (e.g., remembering simple UI preferences). These cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Analytics cookies (if used). Limited, privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate usage. Where required, we will request your consent and respect your choices.
  • Local storage. Some calculators may use local storage to remember your last inputs or units. This never leaves your device and can be cleared in your browser settings.

Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control: If detected, we treat this as an opt-out signal for non-essential analytics.

4) Third-Party Services

We may use third-party vendors for:

  • Font delivery (e.g., Google Fonts)
  • Anonymous analytics (privacy-minded measurement)
  • Content delivery (CDN for speed and reliability)

These providers may process limited technical data necessary to deliver their services. We do not sell your data.

5) Data Security

We employ reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6) Data Retention

We retain only what is necessary to operate the service:

  • Local calculator inputs: stored on your device only (you can clear them anytime)
  • Server logs: retained for a limited period (typically up to 30–90 days) for security and troubleshooting, unless longer retention is required by law
  • Aggregate analytics: retained to analyze trends without identifying individuals

7) Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction/objection to processing, and withdrawal of consent. Because we collect minimal information and generally avoid personal data, there is often no identifiable data to action. You can also:

  • Clear cookies and local storage in your browser
  • Adjust analytics/cookie consent (where presented)
  • Send a request to contact@calcdomain.com

EEA/UK users may lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. California and other US state residents can exercise applicable rights under local laws; we do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

8) Children’s Privacy

CalcDomain is intended for a general audience and not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child provided personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

9) International Transfers

Our infrastructure and third-party providers may operate globally. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will appear on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Material changes may include additional notice.

11) Contact

Questions or requests? Email us at contact@calcdomain.com or visit our Contact page.
