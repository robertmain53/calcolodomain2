The Formula Explained

\[ \text{Tax Owed} = \text{Income} \times \text{Tax Rate} \]

Glossary of Terms

  • Annual Income: Total earnings for the year before taxes.
  • Municipal Tax Rate: The percentage of income levied as tax by the municipality.
  • Total Tax Owed: The total tax amount payable based on the municipal tax rate.

How It Works: An Example Step-by-Step

Assume an annual income of $50,000 in a municipality with a tax rate of 2%. The tax owed would be calculated as follows:

Tax Owed = $50,000 × 0.02 = $1,000
