Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the ideal TV size for my room?

The ideal TV size depends on the room's dimensions and the viewing distance. Use our calculator for precise recommendations.

How far should I sit from my TV?

The optimal viewing distance is typically calculated based on the TV size. Our calculator provides a quick and accurate measurement.

Does the TV type affect the viewing distance?

Yes, factors like resolution and display technology can influence viewing distance. However, our calculator provides a general guideline.

Can I use this calculator for projectors?

While primarily designed for TVs, the calculator can provide a rough estimate for projectors, considering similar viewing angles.

What if my room is irregularly shaped?

For irregularly shaped rooms, consider the longest viewing distance and adjust accordingly using the calculator.
