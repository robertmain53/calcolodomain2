Glossary of Terms

  • Bit: The smallest unit of data in a computer, representing a binary value of 0 or 1.
  • Byte: Consists of 8 bits, historically used to encode a single character of text.
  • KB, MB, GB, TB, PB: Larger units of data storage, representing kilobytes, megabytes, gigabytes, terabytes, and petabytes respectively.
