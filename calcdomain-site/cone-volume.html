The Formula Explained

For a right circular cone, the volume is:

$$ V = \frac{1}{3}\,\pi r^{2} h $$

If you use diameter d instead of radius r, then r = d/2 and:

$$ V = \frac{1}{3}\,\pi \left(\frac{d}{2}\right)^{2} h = \frac{\pi d^{2} h}{12} $$
