Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why is hydration important?

Hydration is crucial for maintaining bodily functions, including temperature regulation, joint lubrication, and nutrient transport.

Can I drink too much water?

While staying hydrated is important, excessive water intake can lead to water intoxication, a dangerous condition that affects the balance of electrolytes in your body.
