Glossary of Terms

  • CFM (Cubic Feet per Minute): A unit of flow rate commonly used in the United States.
  • LPS (Liters per Second): A metric unit of flow rate.

Example Calculation

To convert 500 CFM to LPS, use the formula: LPS = 500 × 0.4719474 = 235.9737 LPS.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is CFM?

CFM stands for Cubic Feet per Minute, a unit to measure the flow rate of air or gas.

How do I convert CFM to LPS?

You can convert CFM to LPS by multiplying the CFM value by 0.4719474.

Why do I need to convert CFM to LPS?

Converting CFM to LPS is useful when working with international systems or when precise flow rate measurements are required.
