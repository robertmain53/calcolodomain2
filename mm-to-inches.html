Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the standard conversion rate of 1 inch = 25.4 millimeters.

The Formula Explained

\( \text{Inches} = \frac{\text{Millimeters}}{25.4} \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Millimeters: A metric unit of length, equal to one thousandth of a meter.
  • Inches: A unit of length in the imperial system, used primarily in the United States.

Practical Example: Step-by-Step

To convert 100 millimeters into inches, divide 100 by 25.4, resulting in approximately 3.937 inches.
