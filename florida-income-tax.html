The Formula Explained

Mathematical formula for Florida state income tax.

We model Florida state income tax as:

LaTeX: $$T_{\text{state}} = r_{\text{state}} \times I_{\text{taxable}}, \quad \text{where } r_{\text{state}} = 0$$

Therefore, for any taxable income I: LaTeX: $$T_{\text{state}} = 0 \times I = 0$$

Glossary of Variables

  • Income Type: Salary or Hourly, used to determine how income is entered and annualized.
  • Pay Frequency: How often salary is paid (weekly, biweekly, semimonthly, monthly, annual).
  • Gross Pay per Period: Total pay before any taxes or deductions for the selected period.
  • Hourly Rate / Hours per Week / Weeks per Year: Inputs used to compute annual income for hourly workers.
  • Florida State Income Tax Rate (r_state): Constant 0%.
  • Per-Period State Income Tax: The portion of state tax for the selected period; always $0 in Florida.
  • Annualized State Income Tax: Yearly total state tax; always $0 in Florida.
  • Effective State Tax Rate: Florida state tax divided by income; always 0%.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does Florida have a state income tax?

No. Florida does not impose a state personal income tax. Your Florida state income tax is $0 regardless of filing status.

Are there local income taxes in Florida?

No. Florida cities and counties do not levy local income taxes on wages.

Which taxes still affect my paycheck in Florida?

Federal income tax and FICA (Social Security and Medicare) apply. Employers also pay Florida reemployment tax (unemployment), but it is not withheld from employees.

Why use this calculator if the rate is 0%?

To validate the official 0% state rate, present authoritative documentation, and quantify that the per‑paycheck and annual state tax impact is $0.

If I live in a state with income tax but work in Florida, what happens?

You may still owe your resident state. Check your resident state’s tax rules or consult a professional.

Is sales tax the same as income tax?

No. Florida has sales and use taxes (including county surtaxes) on purchases, but that is separate from income tax on wages, which is 0%.
