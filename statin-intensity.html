Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is statin intensity?

Statin intensity refers to the expected percentage lowering of LDL cholesterol achieved by a specific statin and dose.

Why is LDL cholesterol important?

LDL cholesterol is a major risk factor for cardiovascular disease, which statins help to reduce.
