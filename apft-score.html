Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the APFT?

The Army Physical Fitness Test measures a soldier's physical readiness through push-ups, sit-ups, and a 2-mile run.

How often should the APFT be taken?

The APFT is typically administered twice a year for active duty soldiers.

What are the benefits of the APFT?

It ensures soldiers maintain physical fitness and readiness for military duties.
