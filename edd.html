The Formula Explained

The formula used for EDD calculation is:

EDD = LMP + 280 days

Glossary of Terms

  • LMP: Last Menstrual Period, the first day of your last period.
  • EDD: Estimated Due Date, the estimated date when the baby will be born.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How accurate is the EDD?

The EDD is an estimate and actual delivery can vary by a couple of weeks.

What if I don't remember my LMP?

Your healthcare provider can estimate the EDD using ultrasound data.

Can the EDD change?

Yes, based on ultrasound findings and other factors.
