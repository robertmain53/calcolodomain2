The Formula Explained

Military time conversion involves adding 12 to any hour past 12:00 PM and removing the "PM" designation, or simply removing "AM" for times before 12:00 PM.

Glossary of Variables

  • Military Time: 24-hour time format used by militaries and other fields.
  • Standard Time: 12-hour time format used in civilian life.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What is military time? A time format that uses 24 hours to divide the day.
  • How do I convert 15:00 military time? 15:00 military time is 3:00 PM in standard time.
  • Why is military time used? It avoids confusion between AM and PM times.
  • Can this tool convert other time zones? This tool focuses on converting between military and standard time within the same time zone.
  • Is this tool free to use? Yes, this conversion tool is completely free.
