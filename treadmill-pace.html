The Formula Explained

The pace is calculated using the formula: Pace = Time / Distance, where time is derived from the speed input.

Glossary of Terms

  • Speed (km/h): The velocity at which the treadmill is set.
  • Distance (km): The total distance covered during the run.
  • Pace (min/km): The time it takes to cover one kilometer, expressed in minutes.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, if you run at a speed of 10 km/h for 5 km, the calculator will determine your pace as 6 minutes per kilometer.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is running pace?

Running pace refers to the time it takes to cover a certain distance, typically measured in minutes per kilometer or mile.

How can this calculator help me?

This calculator can help you plan your workouts by providing accurate pace metrics based on your treadmill settings.

Is pace important for training?

Yes, understanding your pace is crucial for setting training goals and tracking progress.
