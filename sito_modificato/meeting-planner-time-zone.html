Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the Time Zone Database provided by IANA. For more information, visit IANA Time Zone Database. All calculations are rigorously based on this source.

The Formula Explained

Time = Input Time + Time Zone Offset

Glossary of Variables

  • Input Time: The initial time entered for conversion.
  • Time Zone Offset: The difference in hours between two time zones.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a time zone?

A time zone is a region of the globe that observes a uniform standard time for legal, commercial, and social purposes.

How accurate is this tool?

The tool is as accurate as the time zone data provided by IANA.
