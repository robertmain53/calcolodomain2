The Formula Explained

The formula for the area of a regular polygon is:
\( A = \frac{n \times s^2}{4 \times \tan(\frac{\pi}{n})} \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Number of Sides (n): The total number of sides in the polygon.
  • Length of Each Side (s): The length of one side of the polygon.
  • Area (A): The total area enclosed by the polygon.

FAQ

How do I use this calculator?

Enter the number of sides and the length of each side, then click 'Calculate Area' to get the result.

What is a regular polygon?

A regular polygon is a polygon with all sides and angles equal.

Can this calculator be used for irregular polygons?

No, this calculator is designed specifically for regular polygons.
