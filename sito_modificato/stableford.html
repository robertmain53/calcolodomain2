Data Source and Methodology

All calculations rely on the official Stableford scoring system, a widely recognized golf scoring method.

The Formula Explained

Stableford points are calculated based on the score achieved on each hole compared to the par for the hole, adjusted for handicap.

Glossary of Terms

  • Score: The number of strokes taken.
  • Course Par: The standard number of strokes set for the course.
  • Stableford Score: The calculated points based on the Stableford system.

FAQs

What is a Stableford score?

It is a scoring method in golf where points are awarded based on the number of strokes taken at each hole.

How is the score calculated?

The score is based on the strokes taken compared to the hole's par, with adjustments for handicap.
