Roman numeral rules

Roman numerals are built from these symbols:

SymbolValueNotes
I1
V5
X10
L50
C100
D500
M1000
IV, IX4, 9Subtractive notation
XL, XC40, 90Subtractive notation
CD, CM400, 900Subtractive notation

This tool enforces standard modern form (no overlines, no 5000+, no non-standard repetitions).
