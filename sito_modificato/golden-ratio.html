Source of Data and Methodology

The golden ratio is derived from the Fibonacci sequence and is represented by the Greek letter phi (φ). It is approximately equal to 1.618033988749895. All calculations are based on this mathematical constant.

The Formula Explained

  • Golden Ratio: The product of the number and the golden ratio constant φ.

The golden ratio is a special number approximately equal to 1.618 that appears frequently in mathematics, art, and architecture.
