Food Calories (kcal) to Kilojoules Converter

This calculator helps you convert food calories, measured in kilocalories (kcal), to kilojoules (kJ). This is useful for those tracking energy intake for dietary purposes.

Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the conversion factor provided by authoritative resources. 1 kcal = 4.184 kJ. For more information, visit UnitConverters.net.

The Formula Explained

The formula used for conversion is:

\( \text{Kilojoules (kJ)} = \text{Kilocalories (kcal)} \times 4.184 \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Kilocalories (kcal): A unit of energy used to describe the energy content of foods.
  • Kilojoules (kJ): An SI unit of energy. 1 kcal is equivalent to 4.184 kJ.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

If you have 100 kcal, convert it to kJ using the formula: \( 100 \times 4.184 = 418.4 \, \text{kJ} \).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a kilocalorie (kcal)?

A kilocalorie is a unit of energy, commonly used to express the energy content in foods.

How do I convert kcal to kJ?

To convert kcal to kJ, multiply the kcal value by 4.184.

Why do we use kJ instead of kcal?

kJ is the SI unit of energy, making it a standard in many countries for nutritional labeling.

Can I use this converter for exercise energy calculations?

Yes, this converter is useful for converting energy values in both food and exercise contexts.

Is this conversion accurate?

The conversion is based on the universally accepted factor of 1 kcal = 4.184 kJ, ensuring accuracy.
