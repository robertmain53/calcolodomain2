Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are strictly based on engineering standards and data from authoritative sources. Refer to ASCE 7-22 for detailed guidelines.

The Formula Explained

Crane Capacity Required = (Load Weight × Safety Factor) / (Distance × Efficiency Factor)

Glossary of Variables

  • Load Weight: The weight of the load to be lifted.
  • Distance: The horizontal distance from the crane to the load.
