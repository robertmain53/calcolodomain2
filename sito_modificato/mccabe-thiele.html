Distillation Column Calculator (McCabe-Thiele)

This calculator assists chemical engineers in determining the number of theoretical stages required for distillation using the McCabe-Thiele method.

Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the principles outlined in the McCabe-Thiele method. Refer to the authoritative sources in chemical engineering literature for more details.

The Formula Explained

Stages = f(Quality, Ratio, ...)

Glossary of Terms

  • Feed Quality: The quality of the feed entering the distillation column.
  • Reflux Ratio: The ratio of liquid returned to the column compared to the liquid taken off as product.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the McCabe-Thiele method?

The McCabe-Thiele method is a graphical design technique for analyzing the number of theoretical stages in distillation.

Why is reflux ratio important?

The reflux ratio determines the efficiency of separation in the distillation process.
