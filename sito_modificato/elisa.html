Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is ELISA?

ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is a plate-based assay technique designed for detecting and quantifying substances such as peptides, proteins, antibodies, and hormones.

How do I interpret ELISA results?

Results are interpreted based on the standard curve and the linear range of the assay. It's crucial to follow the protocol and compare results with control samples.
