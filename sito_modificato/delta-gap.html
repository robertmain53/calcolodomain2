The Formula Explained

\(\Delta \text{Gap} = \text{Anion Gap} - \text{Delta Bicarbonate}\)

Glossary of Variables

  • Anion Gap: The difference between measured cations and anions in the blood.
  • Delta Bicarbonate: The change in bicarbonate concentration.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the anion gap?

The anion gap is a measurement of the difference between positively charged ions and negatively charged ions in the blood.

Why is the delta gap important?

The delta gap helps to assess the degree of metabolic acidosis and can guide treatment plans.
