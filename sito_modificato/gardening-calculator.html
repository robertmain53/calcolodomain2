Authoritative Data Source & Methodology

AuthoritativeDataSource: Standard geometric volume and packing relations, unit conversions (NIST Guide to SI), and hexagonal packing density from classical circle packing in a plane.

  • NIST, Guide for the Use of the International System of Units (SI) , conversions between ft–in–yd–m–cm–L–m³. Direct reference: NIST SI Units
  • Hexagonal packing surface efficiency \( \eta_{\mathrm{hex}}=\frac{\pi}{2\sqrt{3}}\approx 0.9069 \) (e.g., circle packing in a plane, MathWorld).

Tutti i calcoli si basano rigorosamente sulle formule e sui dati forniti da questa fonte.

The Formula Explained

Volume (rectangular bed): \( V = L \times W \times D \).

Unit conversions: \( 1\,\text{ft}^3=28.3168466\,\text{L} \), \( 1\,\text{yd}^3=27\,\text{ft}^3 \), \( 1\,\text{m}^3=1000\,\text{L} \).

Bag count : \( \text{Bags}=\left\lceil \dfrac{V}{V_{\text{bag}}} \right\rceil \).

Plant count (square grid) (with area \(A=L\times W\), spacing \(s\)): \( N_{\square} \approx \left\lfloor \dfrac{A}{s^2} \right\rfloor \).

Plant count (hex packing) : \( N_{\hexagon} \approx \left\lfloor \dfrac{\eta_{\mathrm{hex}}\,A}{s^2} \right\rfloor \), where \( \eta_{\mathrm{hex}}\approx0.9069 \).

Blend components for total volume \(V\) and percentages \(p_i\): \( V_i = p_i \times V \).

Come Funziona: Un Esempio Passo-Passo

Scenario: Raised bed 6 ft × 3 ft × 10 in depth. Metric bags of 50 L, add 10% for settling.

  1. Convert depth: 10 in = 0.8333 ft. \(V=6\times3\times0.8333 \approx 15\,\text{ft}^3\).
  2. Convert volume: \(15\,\text{ft}^3 \approx 15\times 28.3168 \approx 425\,\text{L}\).
  3. Add 10%: \(425\times1.10 \approx 468\,\text{L}\).
  4. Bags: \(468/50 \approx 9.36 \Rightarrow 10\) bags.
  5. Plant spacing 30 cm, hex packing on area \(6\text{ ft} \approx 1.8288\text{ m}\), \(3\text{ ft}\approx0.9144\text{ m}\), \(A\approx1.673\,\text{m}^2=16730\,\text{cm}^2\). \(N_{\hexagon}\approx \left\lfloor 0.9069\times 16730 / 30^2 \right\rfloor \approx 16\) plants.

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I include the bed walls in dimensions?

Use inside dimensions—the space that actually holds soil or mulch.

How much extra should I order for settling?

10–20% is common for fresh mixes; wood-heavy mixes may settle more.

Is hex spacing always better?

Only when airflow/disease pressure allows. In humid climates, square spacing may reduce crowding.

Can I change bag units?

Yes. Switch between liters and cubic feet; bag count updates instantly.

Does the calculator support circular beds?

This version targets rectangles. For circles: \(V=\pi r^2 D\). We’ll add a circle mode soon.
