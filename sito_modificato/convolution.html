The Formula Explained

Convolution formula: \( (f * g)(t) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(\tau)g(t - \tau)d\tau \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Function 1: The first function in the convolution.
  • Function 2: The second function which is convolved with the first.
  • Convolution: A mathematical operation that expresses how the shape of one function is modified by another.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is convolution?

Convolution is a mathematical operation used in signal processing to determine the effect of a system on a signal.

How do I calculate convolution?

You can calculate convolution by integrating the product of two functions after one is reversed and shifted.
