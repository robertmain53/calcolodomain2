Use our HSA calculator to estimate your potential savings and tax benefits. This tool is designed for individuals looking to maximize their health savings account benefits and plan their financial future effectively.

Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on standard financial formulas for Health Savings Accounts. For detailed guidelines, please refer to the IRS Publication 969. All calculations strictly adhere to the formulas and data provided by this source.

The Formula Explained

Estimated Savings = (Annual Contribution + Current Balance) × (1 + Interest Rate)

Glossary of Terms

  • Annual Contribution: The amount you plan to contribute to your HSA each year.
  • Current HSA Balance: The amount currently held in your HSA.
  • Estimated Savings: The total projected savings in your HSA, considering both contributions and interest.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is an HSA?

An HSA is a savings account that lets you set aside money on a pre-tax basis to pay for qualified medical expenses.

How much can I contribute to an HSA?

Contribution limits change annually. For the latest limits, consult the IRS guidelines or your financial advisor.

Are HSA contributions tax-deductible?

Yes, contributions made to an HSA are tax-deductible up to the annual contribution limit.

Can I invest my HSA funds?

Yes, many HSA providers offer investment options which can allow your funds to grow tax-free.

What happens to my HSA if I change jobs?

Your HSA is portable. You can take it with you when you change jobs or retire.
