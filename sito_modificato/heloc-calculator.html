Formula (extracted text)
Draw (interest-only): \\( \\, \mathrm{Payment}_{draw} = B_0 \\times \\frac{r}{12} + \\frac{\\text{AnnualFee}}{12} \\) Repayment (amortization): \\( \\, P = B_{end\\,draw} \\cdot \\dfrac{r_m}{1-(1+r_m)^{-n}} \\), where \\( r_m = \\frac{\\text{APR}}{12} \\) and \\( n = 12\\times\\text{years} \\). CLTV credit limit: \\(\\, \\text{Max Credit} = \\text{MaxCLTV}\\% \\times V - M \\).
Variables and units
  • P = principal (loan amount) (currency)
  • r = periodic interest rate (annual rate ÷ payments per year) (1)
  • n = total number of payments (years × payments per year) (count)
  • M = periodic payment for principal + interest (currency)
