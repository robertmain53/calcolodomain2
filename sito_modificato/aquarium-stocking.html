Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based strictly on the guidelines provided by the American Society of Ichthyologists and Herpetologists. Refer to their publication for detailed information.

The Formula Explained

For small fish: Fish Count = Tank Volume / 5

For medium fish: Fish Count = Tank Volume / 10

For large fish: Fish Count = Tank Volume / 20

Glossary of Terms

  • Tank Volume: The total water capacity of the aquarium.
  • Fish Type: Category of fish size used for calculation.
