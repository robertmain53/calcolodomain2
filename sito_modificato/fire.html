Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based strictly on the IRS guidelines and standard retirement planning formulas. Consult with a financial advisor for personalized advice.

The Formula Explained

Years to Retirement: Years = (Total Savings Needed - Current Savings) / Annual Savings

Glossary of Terms

  • Current Savings: The total amount of money you currently have saved.
  • Annual Savings: The amount of money you add to your savings each year.
  • Expected Return Rate: The annual percentage return you expect on your investments.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the FIRE method?

The FIRE method is a way to achieve financial independence and retire early.

How does the expected return rate affect my retirement?

Higher return rates can significantly reduce the time needed to reach your retirement savings goal.
