How to use

Enter your round score, the official course rating, and the slope rating. The calculator applies the USGA handicap formula and updates instantly or after pressing Calculate.

Methodology

The handicap index is based on the USGA system: (Score - Course Rating) × 113 / Slope Rating. Rounded to one decimal place, it represents your playing potential relative to a scratch golfer.
