Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are strictly based on the USGA Handicap System, ensuring reliable and accurate results. Learn more about USGA.

The Formula Explained

The formula used for calculating the golf handicap is: Handicap Index = (Score - Course Rating) × 113 / Slope Rating.

Glossary of Terms

  • Score: Your total score for the round.
  • Course Rating: An evaluation of the playing difficulty of a course for a scratch golfer.
  • Slope Rating: A measure of the relative difficulty of a course for a bogey golfer compared to a scratch golfer.
