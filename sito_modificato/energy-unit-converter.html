Glossary of Variables

  • Joules (J): The SI unit of energy.
  • Calories (cal): A unit of energy commonly used in nutrition.
  • Kilowatt Hours (kWh): A unit of energy commonly used to measure electricity consumption.
  • British Thermal Units (BTU): A unit of heat energy.
