The Formula Explained

eDPI = DPI × Sensitivity

Glossary of Terms

  • DPI: Dots Per Inch, a measure of mouse sensitivity.
  • In-Game Sensitivity: The sensitivity setting within a game.
  • eDPI: Effective DPI, calculated as DPI multiplied by in-game sensitivity.

Example Use Case

For a mouse with DPI of 800 and an in-game sensitivity of 0.5, the eDPI would be:

eDPI = 800 × 0.5 = 400

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is eDPI?

eDPI stands for effective DPI and is calculated by multiplying your mouse DPI by your in-game sensitivity.

Why is eDPI important?

eDPI provides a standardized measure of sensitivity that can be compared across different setups.
