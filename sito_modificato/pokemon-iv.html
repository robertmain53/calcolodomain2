The Formula Explained

IV Formula: IV = ((Stat / Base) * 100) - 5

Glossary of Terms

  • Level: The level of your Pokémon.
  • Stats: The current stats of your Pokémon.
  • IV: Individual Values, representing the Pokémon's potential.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are Individual Values (IVs)?

IVs are hidden stats that determine how strong a Pokémon can become.

Why are IVs important?

IVs help you understand the potential maximum stats of your Pokémon.

How is the IV calculated?

The calculator uses your Pokémon's current stats and level to estimate its IVs.
