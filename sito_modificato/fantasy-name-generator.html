Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a Fantasy Name Generator?

A tool to generate unique names for characters in fantasy settings.

How does the generator work?

The generator uses predefined name parts and random algorithms to create names.

Yes, the names are unique and free to use in your creative works.

No, you can generate as many names as you need for your project.
