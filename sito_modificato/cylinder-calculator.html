Formula (extracted text)
For a right circular cylinder with radius \(r\) and height \(h\): Volume: \[ V=\pi r^2 h \] Lateral surface: \[ A_L = 2\pi r h \] Total surface: \[ A_T = 2\pi r (r+h) = A_L + 2\pi r^2 \] Base area: \[ A_B = \pi r^2 \] Circumference: \[ C = 2\pi r \] If density \( \rho \) is given, mass: \[ m = \rho \, V \]
