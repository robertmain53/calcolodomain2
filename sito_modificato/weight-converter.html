The Formula Explained

Conversion Formula: Target Weight = Source Weight × Conversion Factor

Glossary of Terms

  • Weight: The mass of an object as measured in a particular unit.
  • Conversion Factor: A numerical factor by which a quantity expressed in one set of units is multiplied to convert it to another set of units.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, to convert 10 pounds to kilograms: 10 lbs × 0.453592 = 4.53592 kg.
