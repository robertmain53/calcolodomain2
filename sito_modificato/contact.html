Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an error in a calculator?

Email us at bugs@calcdomain.com with details about the calculator, your inputs, the expected result, and what you actually received. Screenshots are helpful. We prioritize bug fixes and typically respond within 12-24 hours.

Can I request a new calculator?

Absolutely! We're always looking to expand our offerings. Use the contact form above or email support@calcdomain.com with your suggestion. Include what you'd like to calculate and why it would be useful. We evaluate all requests and prioritize based on user demand and our team's expertise.

Do you offer custom calculator development?

We focus on publicly available free tools, but we're open to discussing partnership opportunities for specialized calculators. Email info@calcdomain.com with details about your needs.

Can I embed your calculators on my website?

Currently, our calculators are not available for embedding. However, you're welcome to link to any of our calculators. For partnership or licensing inquiries, contact info@calcdomain.com .

Are your calculators reviewed by professionals?

Yes! Every calculator is built and reviewed by qualified professionals in the relevant field. Our team includes engineers, MBAs, physicians, certified appraisers, and healthcare professionals. Learn more on our About page .

How often are calculators updated?

We review all calculators daily. When regulations change (like IRS contribution limits or mortgage rates), we update immediately‚Äîoften within hours of official announcements.

Do you provide financial advice?

No. We provide educational tools and calculators, not personalized financial, medical, or legal advice. Our calculators offer estimates to help you make informed decisions, but you should always consult qualified professionals for advice specific to your situation.

How do you protect my privacy?

We don't store your calculation data. All computations happen in your browser. We don't track, sell, or share personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for complete details.
