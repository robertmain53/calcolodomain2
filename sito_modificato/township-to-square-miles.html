The Formula Explained

Glossary of Terms

  • Township: A unit of land area used in the United States.
  • Square Mile: A unit of area equal to one mile by one mile square.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a township?

A township is a unit of land area used in the United States, typically representing a 6-mile by 6-mile square.

How do you convert townships to square miles?

Multiply the number of townships by 36 to get the equivalent area in square miles.
