Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Köppen climate classification?

The Köppen climate classification is a widely used system for classifying the world's climates based on average temperature and precipitation.

How do I use the Köppen Climate Classification Calculator?

Input the monthly average temperatures and precipitation levels to determine the climate classification.

Can I use this tool for any location?

Yes, as long as you have the average temperature and precipitation data for that location.

What are the main climate types in the Köppen system?

The main types are tropical, dry, temperate, continental, and polar.
