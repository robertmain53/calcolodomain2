Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on standard mathematical formulas used in gaming statistics. "Total Damage" divided by "Time Taken" provides your DPS.

The Formula Explained

\( \text{DPS} = \frac{\text{Total Damage}}{\text{Time Taken}} \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Total Damage: The cumulative damage dealt within a time frame.
  • Time Taken: The duration over which the damage was dealt, measured in seconds.
  • DPS: Damage Per Second, an indicator of damage efficiency.

FAQs

What is DPS in gaming?

DPS stands for Damage Per Second, a metric used to measure the damage output of a character or weapon in games.

How is DPS calculated?

DPS is calculated by dividing the total damage by the time taken to deal that damage.
