Formula

The conversion from kilograms to pounds uses the following formula:

\[ \text{lbs} = \text{kg} \times 2.20462 \]

This conversion factor is derived from the exact definition: 1 kg = 2.20462262185 lbs.

Data Source and Methodology

This converter uses the internationally accepted conversion between kilograms and pounds:

  • 1 kilogram = 2.20462262185 pounds (exact)
  • This is based on the international avoirdupois pound definition
  • Used worldwide in commerce, science, and everyday applications
  • For practical purposes, 2.20462 provides sufficient accuracy

Step-by-Step Example

Problem: Convert 70 kilograms to pounds.

Step 1: Write down the formula

\[ \text{lbs} = \text{kg} \times 2.20462 \]

Step 2: Substitute the value

\[ \text{lbs} = 70 \times 2.20462 \]

Step 3: Calculate the result

\[ \text{lbs} = 154.32 \text{ lbs} \]

Answer: 70 kilograms = 154.32 pounds

Glossary of Terms

Kilogram (kg)
The base unit of mass in the International System of Units (SI). Used globally as the standard unit of mass.
Pound (lb or lbs)
A unit of weight/mass commonly used in the United States and other countries. Equal to approximately 0.453592 kilograms.
Avoirdupois Pound
The standard pound used for most purposes. This converter uses the avoirdupois pound (as opposed to the troy pound).

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert kilograms to pounds?

Multiply the number of kilograms by 2.20462 to get the equivalent weight in pounds. For example: 50 kg × 2.20462 = 110.23 pounds.

Why do I need a converter?

A converter ensures accuracy and saves time when dealing with weight conversions, especially when traveling between countries that use different measurement systems or when following international recipes.

What's the difference between kg and lbs?

Kilograms (kg) are the metric unit of mass, used worldwide in science and most countries. Pounds (lbs) are an imperial unit of weight, primarily used in the United States. One pound is lighter than one kilogram.

Is the conversion exact?

The conversion factor 2.20462 is rounded for practical use. The exact value is 2.20462262185 pounds per kilogram. For most purposes, using 2.20462 provides sufficient accuracy.

Can I convert pounds back to kilograms?

Yes! To convert pounds to kilograms, divide the pound value by 2.20462 (or multiply by 0.453592). For example: 150 lbs ÷ 2.20462 = 68.04 kg.
