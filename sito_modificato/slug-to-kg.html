Slug to kg Converter

Convert slugs to kilograms and kilograms to slugs using the standard engineering definition: 1 slug = 14.593902937 kg. This is useful when you’re moving from Imperial/US customary mechanics to SI-based calculations.

Formula

kg = slug × 14.593902937

slug = kg ÷ 14.593902937

This factor comes from: 1 slug is the mass that accelerates 1 ft/s² when acted on by 1 lbf; using standard gravity and lbm definitions you get 1 slug ≈ 32.174 lbm ≈ 14.5939 kg.

Example

You have 3.2 slugs:

3.2 × 14.593902937 = 46.700... kg

To go backwards, 50 kg ÷ 14.593902937 = 3.426 slug

When do I see “slug”?

In some engineering textbooks or aerospace problems that use feet, seconds, and pounds-force, the slug is used to make F = m × a “work nicely”. If you need to report in SI, just plug the slug value here and get kg.
