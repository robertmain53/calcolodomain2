The Formula Explained

Land Transfer Tax Formula: Tax = Purchase Price × Rate

Glossary of Terms

  • Purchase Price: The total amount paid for the property.
  • State/Province: The location of the property which determines the tax rate.
  • Land Transfer Tax: The amount of tax payable based on the purchase price and location.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is land transfer tax?

It's a tax imposed by the government on the transfer of property ownership.

How is the tax rate determined?

The tax rate is determined by the state or province where the property is located.
