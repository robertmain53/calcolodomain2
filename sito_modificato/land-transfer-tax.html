How to use

Input the property purchase price, choose the province that governs the transfer, and click Calculate. The results show the tax owed based on the fixed rate for that jurisdiction.

Methodology

The tool multiplies the purchase price by the selected jurisdictional rate (Ontario 2.00%, British Columbia 1.50%). The percent rate reflects simplified brackets from official transfer tax schedules.

Introduction

Estimate land transfer tax on a property purchase using state or provincial brackets. Enter the purchase price and location to see the tax due.

Glossary of Terms

  • Purchase Price: The total amount paid for the property.
  • State/Province: The location of the property which determines the tax rate.
  • Land Transfer Tax: The amount of tax payable based on the purchase price and location.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is land transfer tax?

It's a tax imposed by the government on the transfer of property ownership.

How is the tax rate determined?

The tax rate is determined by the state or province where the property is located.

Additional guidance

A state/province specific calculator keeps the assumptions clear; always refer to local authorities for the final rate.
