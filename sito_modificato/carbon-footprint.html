The Formula Explained

Transportation: \( \text{CO}_2 = \text{miles/week} \times 52 \times \text{emission factor} \)

Electricity: \( \text{CO}_2 = \text{kWh/month} \times 12 \times \text{emission factor} \)

Gas: \( \text{CO}_2 = \text{therms/month} \times 12 \times \text{emission factor} \)

Glossary of Variables

  • Transportation: Miles driven per week using various vehicles.
  • Electricity: Amount of electricity consumed in kilowatt-hours per month.
  • Natural Gas: Therms of natural gas used per month.

FAQ

What is a carbon footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases generated by our actions.

How can I reduce my carbon footprint?

You can reduce your carbon footprint by minimizing energy use, driving less, and recycling more.
