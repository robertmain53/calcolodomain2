Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does the calculator determine the critical load?

The calculator uses the Euler's buckling formula, considering material properties and dimensions.

What is the significance of the safety factor?

The safety factor indicates the margin of safety applied to the column's critical load to prevent failure.

Can this tool be used for aluminum columns?

Yes, by selecting 'Aluminum' as the material, the tool adjusts its calculations accordingly.

Is online data storage required?

No, this tool operates entirely in your browser, ensuring privacy and data security.

What standards support this calculator?

This calculator is based on the AISC standards for structural steel design.
