What is a lorem ipsum generator?

              

                A lorem ipsum generator creates placeholder (dummy) text that
                looks like natural language but carries no real meaning.
                Designers, developers, and content teams use it to test layouts,
                typography, and components before final copy is ready.
              

              

                This tool goes beyond basic paragraphs: you can generate
                sentences, words, headings, bullet lists, and even HTML-ready
                markup with one click.

Best practices for using lorem ipsum

              
1. Use it only during design and development

              

                Lorem ipsum is perfect for early prototypes and internal demos,
                but it should always be replaced with meaningful copy before
                launch. Shipping placeholder text can confuse users and harm
                trust.
              


              
2. Match the length of real content

              

                Try to approximate the real amount of text you expect in
                production. If your product titles are usually 3–6 words, don’t
                design around 15-word headings. Use the “Max words per unit”
                option to keep things realistic.
              


              
3. Consider accessibility and SEO

              

                Screen readers and search engines cannot interpret lorem ipsum.
                For public pages, always replace dummy text with descriptive,
                human-readable content before indexing or user testing.
              


              
FAQ


              
Can I use this lorem ipsum generator commercially?

              

                Yes. Lorem ipsum text is generic placeholder content and is safe
                to use in commercial design work, prototypes, and internal
                tools. Just remember to replace it with real copy before going
                live.
              


              
Why does the text look like broken Latin?

              

                Traditional lorem ipsum is derived from a scrambled passage of
                Cicero’s “De finibus bonorum et malorum”. Over time it has been
                modified and expanded into a pseudo-Latin word pool that looks
                like real language without being readable.
              


              
Is the generated text stored anywhere?

              

                No. All generation happens in your browser using JavaScript.
                Nothing is sent to a server, and your text is not logged or
                stored.
