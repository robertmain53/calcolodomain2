Formula: LPS = GPM × 0.0630902

Glossary of Terms

  • GPM: Gallons per Minute, a unit of flow rate in the US customary units.
  • LPS: Liters per Second, a unit of flow rate in the metric system.

Example Calculation

If you have a flow rate of 50 GPM, using the formula, the conversion to LPS would be:

LPS = 50 × 0.0630902 = 3.15451
