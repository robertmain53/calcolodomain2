Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are strictly based on the guidelines provided by the relevant legal frameworks and statistical data.

The Formula Explained

\( \text{Alimony} = \left(\text{Income} - \text{Expenses}\right) \times \text{Duration Factor} \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Income: Total monthly income from all sources.
  • Expenses: Total monthly living expenses.
  • Duration Factor: A coefficient reflecting the length of the marriage.
