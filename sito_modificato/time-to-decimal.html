Frequently Asked Questions

How does this converter work?

Enter the number of hours and minutes to get the equivalent time in decimal hours.

Why use decimal hours?

Decimal hours are easier to compute with, especially for billing and payroll calculations.

Can I use this calculator for payroll?

Yes, this tool is perfect for converting time worked into decimal format for payroll processing.

What is the formula used?

The formula used is: Decimal Hours = Hours + (Minutes / 60).

What if I enter invalid data?

The calculator will alert you to correct any mistakes before providing a result.
