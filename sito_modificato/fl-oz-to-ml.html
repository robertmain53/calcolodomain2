mL → UK fl oz: \( \text{fl oz}_\text{UK} = \text{mL} \div 28.4130625 \)

Why two different fl oz?

The US and UK systems define the fluid ounce differently. If you follow a recipe, check if it is US-based (most modern web recipes) or UK/Commonwealth/older. This tool outputs both so you can compare.
