The Formula Explained

ΔT = T_final - T_initial

Glossary of Terms

  • Initial Temperature: The starting temperature in Celsius.
  • Final Temperature: The ending temperature in Celsius.
  • Thermal Action: The calculated effect of temperature change on a structure.
