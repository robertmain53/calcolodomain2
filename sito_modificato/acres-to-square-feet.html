How to use

Use either the dimensions inputs to compute area, or enter a value in any unit field to convert between acres, ft², m², hectares, and yd².

Methodology

Conversions are based on the standard factor of 43,560 square feet per acre, with standard unit conversions for m², ha, and yd².

Full original guide (expanded)

To convert from acres to square feet, multiply by 43,560. To convert from square feet to acres, divide by 43,560.

Formulas

Acres to ft²: \( \text{ft}^2 = \text{acres} \times 43{,}560 \)

ft² to acres: \( \text{acres} = \text{ft}^2 / 43{,}560 \)
