Glossary of Terms

  • Bet Type: The type of bet placed in roulette, such as straight, split, etc.
  • Bet Amount: The amount of money wagered on a specific bet.
  • Odds of Winning: The probability of winning based on the bet type.
  • Potential Payout: The expected payout if the bet wins.

Example: How It Works

For a straight bet on a single number, if you bet $10, the odds are 1 in 37 (European roulette), and the potential payout is $350.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the house edge in roulette?

The house edge in European roulette is 2.7%, while it's 5.26% in American roulette due to the extra double zero.

How do I calculate my odds in roulette?

Use our calculator by selecting the type of bet and entering the amount to see your odds and potential payout.

Can I use this calculator for American roulette?

Yes, but remember that the odds differ due to the additional double zero in American roulette.
