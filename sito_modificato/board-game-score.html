How to Use the Score Calculator

Enter the final score for each competing player above. The calculator instantly adds the values to produce the aggregate total so you can resolve ties and confirm standings faster.

Methodology

This calculator follows the standard scoring approach used in most board games: each player's earned points are added with no weighting or modifiers. The summary highlights both the total and the gap so you can judge how close the contest was.

Glossary of Terms

  • Player Score: The score achieved by a player during the game.
  • Total Score: The sum of all players' scores.
