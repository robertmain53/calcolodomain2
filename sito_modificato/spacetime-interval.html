Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a spacetime interval?

A spacetime interval is the measure of separation between two events in space and time, invariant under Lorentz transformations.

Why is the spacetime interval important?

It is a key concept in the theory of relativity, providing a way to describe the geometric structure of spacetime.
