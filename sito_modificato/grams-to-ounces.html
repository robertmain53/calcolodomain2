Formula

The conversion from grams to ounces uses the following formula:

\[ \text{oz} = \text{g} \times 0.03527396 \]

This conversion factor is exact and based on the international avoirdupois ounce definition.

Data Source and Methodology

This converter uses the internationally accepted conversion between grams and ounces:

  • 1 gram = 0.03527396 ounces
  • Equivalently: 1 ounce = 28.3495 grams
  • Based on the international avoirdupois ounce definition
  • Used worldwide in cooking, science, and commerce

Step-by-Step Example

Problem: Convert 100 grams to ounces.

Step 1: Write down the formula

\[ \text{oz} = \text{g} \times 0.03527396 \]

Step 2: Substitute the value

\[ \text{oz} = 100 \times 0.03527396 \]

Step 3: Calculate the result

\[ \text{oz} = 3.527 \text{ oz} \]

Answer: 100 grams = 3.53 ounces

Glossary of Terms

Gram (g)
A metric unit of mass equal to one-thousandth of a kilogram. Commonly used worldwide for measuring weight.
Ounce (oz)
A unit of weight commonly used in the United States and the United Kingdom. Equal to approximately 28.35 grams.
Avoirdupois Ounce
The standard ounce used in everyday measurements (as opposed to the troy ounce used for precious metals).

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert grams to ounces?

To convert grams to ounces, multiply the gram value by 0.03527396. For example: 100 grams × 0.03527396 = 3.527 ounces.

What is a gram?

A gram is a unit of mass in the metric system, equal to one-thousandth of a kilogram. It's commonly used worldwide for measuring weight.

What is an ounce?

An ounce is a unit of weight commonly used in the United States and the United Kingdom. 1 ounce equals approximately 28.35 grams.

How precise is the conversion?

The conversion factor (0.03527396) is accurate to 8 decimal places, ensuring precise results for most practical applications.

Can I convert ounces back to grams?

Yes, you can convert ounces to grams by dividing the ounce value by 0.03527396 (or multiply by 28.3495).
