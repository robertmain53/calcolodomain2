Grams to Ounces Converter

Convert grams (g) to ounces (oz) instantly. Supports standard (avoirdupois) ounces for cooking, shipping and daily use, plus troy ounces for precious metals. Bidirectional: enter grams or ounces and get the other value right away.

Enter any mass in grams.

Rounded to 4 decimals for practicality.

Note

For everyday US/UK use, choose Standard / Avoirdupois. Use Troy only for metals like gold or silver.

Grams to ounces formula

Standard / Avoirdupois:

oz = g ÷ 28.349523125

Troy ounce:

troy oz = g ÷ 31.1034768

To go backwards:

g = oz × 28.349523125 (standard)

g = troy oz × 31.1034768 (troy)

How to convert grams to ounces (step-by-step)

  1. Identify the ounce type: standard (most common) or troy (precious metals).
  2. Take your weight in grams.
  3. Divide by the grams-per-ounce value: 28.3495 for standard, 31.1035 for troy.
  4. Round to 2–4 decimals depending on how accurate you need to be.

Example: Convert 180 g to ounces (standard) → 180 ÷ 28.349523125 = 6.349 oz → round to 6.35 oz.

FAQs

Is 1 oz exactly 28 g?

Not exactly. 1 oz (standard, avoirdupois) = 28.349523125 g. Many cooking sites simplify it to 28 g for convenience.

What ounce should I use for recipes?

Use the standard/avoirdupois ounce. Troy ounce is not for cooking.

Can I convert ounces to grams with this tool?

Yes. Click “Swap direction (oz → g)” and type into the ounces field.

Why do different websites show slightly different values?

Because of rounding. We use the precise NIST factors, so your conversions stay consistent across measurements.
