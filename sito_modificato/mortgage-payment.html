Formula & Calculation Logic

Fixed-Rate Amortization Formula:

\[ M = P \cdot \frac{r(1+r)^{n}}{(1+r)^{n}-1} \]
  • $P$: Principal Loan Amount
  • $r$: Periodic Interest Rate (Annual Rate / Payments per Year)
  • $n$: Total Number of Payments
