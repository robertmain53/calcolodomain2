Texas Local Sales Tax Calculator

This calculator helps you determine the amount of sales tax added to a purchase in Texas. It's perfect for both consumers and businesses to ensure accurate billing.

Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the standard Texas sales tax rate as provided by the Texas Comptroller. All calculations are rigorously based on these formulas and data.

The Formula Explained

Sales Tax Calculation: Tax = PurchaseAmount × (Tax Rate / 100)

Total Amount: Total = PurchaseAmount + Tax

Glossary of Terms

  • Purchase Amount: The total amount before tax.
  • Tax Rate: The percentage of sales tax applied.
  • Total Sales Tax: The amount of tax added to the purchase.
  • Total Amount: The final amount including sales tax.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is sales tax?

Sales tax is a tax imposed by the government on the sale of goods and services.

How is sales tax calculated in Texas?

The sales tax in Texas is calculated by multiplying the purchase amount by the sales tax rate.

What is the current sales tax rate in Texas?

The standard sales tax rate in Texas is 6.25% but local jurisdictions can add up to 2%.
