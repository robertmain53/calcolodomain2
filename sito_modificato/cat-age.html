Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How accurate is the cat age calculator?

The calculator uses a standard conversion formula to approximate the equivalent human age.

Why is the first year so significant?

The first year is significant because cats age rapidly during their initial years, developing quickly in their growth and maturity.

Does this calculator account for different cat breeds?

No, this calculator provides a general estimate and does not account for specific breed differences.
