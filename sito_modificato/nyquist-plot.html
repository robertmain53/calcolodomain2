The Formula Explained

The Nyquist plot is a parametric plot of a frequency response used to assess the stability of a control system.

Glossary of Terms

  • Numerator Coefficients: Coefficients of the numerator of the transfer function.
  • Denominator Coefficients: Coefficients of the denominator of the transfer function.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a Nyquist plot?

A Nyquist plot is used in control systems to assess stability by plotting the frequency response of a system.
