mcg to mg Converter

Convert micrograms (mcg / µg) to milligrams (mg) and milligrams to micrograms. This is especially useful for medical doses, supplements, and laboratory calculations. Remember: 1 mg = 1000 mcg.

How to convert mcg to mg

Micrograms are one thousandth of a milligram. So:

mg = mcg ÷ 1000

mcg = mg × 1000

Example

1350 mcg ÷ 1000 = 1.35 mg

2 mg × 1000 = 2000 mcg

Medical / pharmacy note

This tool is useful for checking unit changes, but medication doses must always follow the prescription or protocol. If your prescription says “mcg” do NOT assume “mg”: mixing them up causes an ×1000 error.
