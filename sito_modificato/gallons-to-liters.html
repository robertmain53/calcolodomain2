The Formula Explained

\( \text{liters} = \text{gallons} \times 3.78541 \)

Where gallons is the volume in US customary units and liters is the volume in the metric system.

Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the conversion factor provided by the NIST: 1 US gallon is approximately 3.78541 liters. Learn more . All calculations are based strictly on the formulas and data provided by this source.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, to convert 5 gallons to liters:

  1. Start with the formula: \( \text{liters} = \text{gallons} \times 3.78541 \)
  2. Substitute 5 for gallons: \( \text{liters} = 5 \times 3.78541 \)
  3. Calculate: \( \text{liters} = 18.92705 \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Gallons (gal): A unit of volume commonly used in the United States. One US gallon equals 3.78541 liters or 128 fluid ounces.
  • Liters (L): A metric unit of volume commonly used worldwide. One liter equals 1,000 cubic centimeters or approximately 0.264 US gallons.
  • US Gallon vs UK Gallon: The US gallon (3.78541 L) is smaller than the Imperial/UK gallon (4.54609 L).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a gallon?

A gallon is a unit of volume measurement used in the United States and some other countries. It is equal to 4 quarts or 3.78541 liters. The US gallon is different from the Imperial gallon used in the UK.

How do I convert gallons to liters?

To convert gallons to liters, multiply the gallon value by 3.78541. For example: 5 gallons × 3.78541 = 18.92705 liters.

Why is it important to convert gallons to liters?

Converting gallons to liters is crucial for scientific calculations, international trade, and when using equipment that uses metric units. Most countries use the metric system, making liter conversions essential for global communication.

Can this calculator be used for both US and UK gallons?

This calculator is specifically designed for US gallons. The conversion factor for UK (Imperial) gallons is different (4.54609 liters per gallon instead of 3.78541).

What is the difference between a US gallon and a UK gallon?

A US gallon is approximately 3.78541 liters, while a UK (Imperial) gallon is approximately 4.54609 liters. The UK gallon is about 20% larger than the US gallon.
