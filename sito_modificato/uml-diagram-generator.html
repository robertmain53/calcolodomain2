Glossary of Variables

  • UML Type: The type of UML diagram to generate (e.g., Class, Sequence).
  • Diagram Details: The specific elements and connections within the diagram.

Example: Step-by-Step

To generate a class diagram, select "Class Diagram" from the dropdown, enter the details of your classes and relationships, and click "Generate Diagram".

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a UML Diagram?

UML (Unified Modeling Language) diagrams are used to represent software architecture and design.

How do I choose the right diagram type?

Choose based on your specific needs: Class for structure, Sequence for process, Use Case for requirements.

Do I need special software to view the diagrams?

No, the diagrams generated can be viewed directly in your browser.

Can I customize the diagram after generating?

Yes, you can modify the details and regenerate as needed.
