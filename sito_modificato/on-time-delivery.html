The Formula Explained

The formula for calculating on-time delivery rate is:

On-Time Delivery Rate = (On-Time Deliveries / Total Deliveries) × 100%

Glossary of Terms

  • Total Deliveries: The total number of deliveries made.
  • On-Time Deliveries: The number of deliveries made on or before the scheduled time.
  • On-Time Delivery Rate: The percentage of deliveries made on time.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

Consider a scenario where you made 100 deliveries in a month, and 90 of those were on time. The on-time delivery rate would be calculated as (90 / 100) × 100% = 90%.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is on-time delivery?

On-time delivery refers to the ability to deliver products to customers at the promised time.

Why is on-time delivery important?

It is crucial for maintaining customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

How can I improve my on-time delivery rate?

Improving logistics planning, optimizing routes, and ensuring clear communication with customers can help improve your rate.
