The Formula Explained

The formula used to calculate your cat's daily caloric needs is:

Calories = 70 × (Weight in kg)^0.75

Glossary of Terms

  • Weight: Your cat's weight in kilograms.
  • Activity Level: The typical activity level of your cat.
  • Calories: The amount of energy your cat needs daily.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

Let's say your cat weighs 4 kg and has a moderate activity level. Plugging into the formula:

Calories = 70 × (4 kg)^0.75 = 200 kcal

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use this calculator?

Simply enter your cat's weight and select their activity level to get the daily calorie needs.

Why is it important to know my cat's calorie needs?

Understanding your cat's calorie needs helps in maintaining a healthy weight and avoiding obesity.

Can I use this calculator for kittens?

This calculator is designed for adult cats. Consult your vet for kitten-specific dietary needs.

What should I do if my cat is overweight?

Consult with your vet to create a safe weight-loss plan tailored to your cat's needs.

Does activity level affect calorie needs?

Yes, more active cats require more calories than those with a sedentary lifestyle.
