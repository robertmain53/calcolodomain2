Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on standard gestation periods for dogs and cats. Please consult with a veterinarian for precise guidance.

The Formula Explained

The expected delivery date is calculated by adding the average gestation period to the mating date. For dogs, this is approximately 63 days, and for cats, approximately 65 days.

Glossary of Terms

  • Mating Date: The date when the animal was bred.
  • Gestation Period: The period of time between conception and delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use the Pet Gestation Calculator?

Simply enter the date of mating and select the type of pet to calculate the expected delivery date.

What pets does this calculator support?

The calculator supports dogs and cats.
