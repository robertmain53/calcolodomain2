Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Karnaugh Map?

A Karnaugh Map is a diagram used in Boolean algebra and digital logic design for the simplification of algebraic expressions.

Who can benefit from this tool?

Students, educators, and professionals in fields such as computer science and electrical engineering can benefit from using this tool.

How does the K-Map solver work?

The K-Map solver takes your logical expression and visualizes it on a Karnaugh Map, providing an optimized solution.
