The Formula Explained

Rest Time = (Set Duration × Intensity Factor)

Glossary of Terms

  • Exercise Intensity: The level of effort required by an exercise, categorized as low, medium, or high.
  • Set Duration: The total time taken to complete a set of exercises.
  • Recommended Rest Time: The optimal time suggested for resting between sets.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How should I choose the exercise intensity?

Choose based on your perceived exertion. Low for light exercises, medium for moderate, and high for intense workouts.

Why is rest time important?

Proper rest time ensures muscle recovery and enhances workout effectiveness by preventing overtraining.

Can I customize the rest time?

Yes, adjust based on your personal fitness level and recovery needs.
