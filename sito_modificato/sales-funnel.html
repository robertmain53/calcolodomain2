Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on standard conversion rate formulas. For more detailed guidance, refer to authoritative marketing analytics sources.

The Formula Explained

Conversion Rate = (Conversions / Total Visitors) × 100

Glossary of Terms

  • Total Visitors: The total number of individuals who visited your funnel.
  • Conversions: The number of visitors who completed the desired action.
  • Conversion Rate: The percentage of visitors who converted.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a sales funnel?

A sales funnel is a marketing concept that maps out the journey a customer goes through when making a purchase decision.

How do I calculate conversion rates?

Conversion rate is calculated by dividing the number of conversions by the total number of visitors and multiplying by 100.
