Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Mayan calendar?

The Mayan calendar is a system of calendars used in pre-Columbian Mesoamerica and in many modern communities in the Guatemalan highlands, Veracruz, Oaxaca, and Chiapas, Mexico.
