How to convert square kilometers to square miles

Square kilometers (km²) and square miles (mi²) are both units used to measure large areas, such as cities, regions, or countries. Square kilometers belong to the metric system, while square miles are part of the imperial/US customary system.

Conversion formulas

From square kilometers to square miles

mi² = km² × 0.386102

1 km² ≈ 0.386102 mi²

From square miles to square kilometers

km² = mi² × 2.58999

1 mi² ≈ 2.58999 km²

Example: 25 km² to square miles

  1. Write down the formula: mi² = km² × 0.386102
  2. Substitute 25 for km²: mi² = 25 × 0.386102
  3. Calculate: 25 × 0.386102 = 9.65255

So, 25 km² ≈ 9.65255 mi².

Example: 50 mi² to square kilometers

  1. Use the reverse formula: km² = mi² × 2.58999
  2. Substitute 50 for mi²: km² = 50 × 2.58999
  3. Calculate: 50 × 2.58999 = 129.4995

So, 50 mi² ≈ 129.4995 km².

Understanding the units

What is a square kilometer?

A square kilometer is the area of a square with each side 1 kilometer long. It is commonly used worldwide in maps, geography, and statistics.

  • 1 km = 1,000 meters
  • 1 km² = 1,000 m × 1,000 m = 1,000,000 m²

What is a square mile?

A square mile is the area of a square with each side 1 mile long. It is widely used in the United States and the UK for land area.

  • 1 mile ≈ 1.609344 kilometers
  • 1 mi² ≈ 2.58999 km²

Where are km² and mi² used?

Typical uses include:

  • Country, state, and province sizes
  • City and county land area
  • National parks and protected areas
  • Large land parcels and agricultural regions

If your data source is from a metric country or scientific report, it will likely use km². If it is from US or UK real estate, government, or media, it may use mi².

FAQ

How many square miles are in a square kilometer?

There are approximately 0.386102 square miles in 1 square kilometer. For most everyday purposes, rounding to 0.3861 is accurate enough.

How many square kilometers are in a square mile?

There are approximately 2.58999 square kilometers in 1 square mile. You can round this to 2.59 km² for quick mental estimates.

Which is larger: 100 km² or 50 mi²?

Convert 50 mi² to km²:

km² = 50 × 2.58999 ≈ 129.4995 km²

So 50 mi² is larger than 100 km².
