Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How reliable are the calculations?

Our calculator is based on AISC standards, which are trusted in the industry for their accuracy and reliability.

Can this calculator be used for all types of composite beams?

While it covers standard cases, for unique beam designs, consult a structural engineer.

What is the significance of the neutral axis?

The neutral axis is crucial for understanding the stress distribution in the beam, which affects its structural performance.
