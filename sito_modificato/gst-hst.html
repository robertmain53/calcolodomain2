GST/HST Calculator

This calculator helps Canadian businesses and consumers quickly determine the GST/HST payable on goods and services. Enter the net amount and select your province to get the GST/HST rate and the total amount payable.

GST/HST Amount: $0.00
Total Amount (CAD): $0.00

Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on official government rates provided by the Canada Revenue Agency. Consult the official CRA website for the most current rates. All calculations are based on these official rates and guidelines.

The Formula Explained

The GST/HST amount is calculated using the formula:

GST/HST Amount = Net Amount × (GST/HST Rate / 100)

Glossary of Terms

  • Net Amount: The price of a good or service before GST/HST is added.
  • GST/HST Rate: The percentage of tax applied, determined by province.
  • GST/HST Amount: The monetary value of the tax.
  • Total Amount: The net amount plus the GST/HST amount.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is GST/HST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) and HST (Harmonized Sales Tax) are consumption taxes in Canada. HST is a combination of GST and provincial sales tax.

How do I know the correct GST/HST rate for my province?

The rates vary by province. Refer to the CRA's official resources or our calculator's dropdown options for the most current rates.

Why is it important to calculate GST/HST?

Accurate calculation ensures compliance with tax regulations and helps in proper financial planning and reporting.

Can this calculator be used for all provinces?

Yes, our calculator includes rates for all Canadian provinces.

Is the calculator result accurate?

All calculations are based on official rates and are accurate as of the latest CRA guidelines. Always verify with official documents for specific cases.
