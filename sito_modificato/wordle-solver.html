Data Source and Methodology

The data used for solving Wordle puzzles is based on extensive dictionary databases and pattern matching algorithms. All calculations are performed rigorously to provide the most accurate word suggestions.

The Formula Explained

The solver uses a pattern matching formula: Match = Dictionary Words - Excluded Letters

Glossary of Terms

  • Known Letters: Letters in their correct positions you know from the puzzle.
  • Excluded Letters: Letters that do not appear in the word.
