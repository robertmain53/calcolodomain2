Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the push-up test?

The push-up test measures upper body muscular endurance by testing how many push-ups an individual can perform in a set time.

How do I perform the push-up test?

Perform as many push-ups as possible in a set time, usually one minute.

Is the push-up test suitable for all ages?

Yes, but modifications may be necessary for different age groups and fitness levels.

How often should I take the push-up test?

It is recommended to perform this test every few months to monitor progress.

Can I improve my push-up test score?

Yes, regular strength training and consistent practice can improve your score.
