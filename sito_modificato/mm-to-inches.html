FAQs

How many mm are in one inch? There are exactly 25.4 millimeters in one inch per the International Yard and Pound Agreement.

Is 10 mm half an inch? No. 10 mm is roughly 0.3937 inches, which is slightly more than 3/8" and just under 13/32".

How do you read mm to inches on a ruler? Compare the mm side to the inches side; 25.4 mm aligns perfectly with 1 inch.
