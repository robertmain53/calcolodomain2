The Conversion Formula

The conversion between inches and centimeters is based on a single, exact value defined by international agreement:

$1 \text{ inch} = 2.54 \text{ centimeters}$

This means that to convert from inches to centimeters, you multiply your inch value by 2.54. Conversely, to convert from centimeters to inches, you divide your centimeter value by 2.54.

  • Formula (in to cm): $cm = inches \times 2.54$
  • Formula (cm to in): $inches = cm \div 2.54$

How to Convert Inches to Centimeters

Follow these simple steps to manually convert any measurement from inches to cm.

  1. Get your measurement in inches. This can be a decimal (like 6.5) or a fraction (like 6 1/2). If it's a fraction, convert it to a decimal first (e.g., 1/2 = 0.5, so 6 1/2 = 6.5).
  2. Multiply by 2.54. Take your decimal inch value and multiply it by 2.54.
  3. The result is your length in centimeters.

Example: Convert 8 inches to cm.

$8 \text{ in} \times 2.54 = 20.32 \text{ cm}$

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is 1 inch in cm?

Exactly 1 inch is equal to 2.54 centimeters (cm). This is the standard, internationally accepted value.

How do you convert 5' 6" (feet and inches) to cm?

To convert feet and inches to cm, you must first convert everything to inches and then multiply by 2.54.

  1. First, convert 5 feet to inches: 5 feet * 12 inches/foot = 60 inches.
  2. Add the remaining 6 inches: 60 in + 6 in = 66 inches.
  3. Convert the total inches to cm: 66 in * 2.54 cm/in = 167.64 cm.

So, 5' 6" is equal to 167.64 cm.

Is 1 cm bigger than 1 inch?

No, 1 inch is bigger than 1 cm. One inch is 2.54 times larger than one centimeter.

Why is an inch exactly 2.54 cm?

This value was standardized by international agreement in 1959. Countries using both the imperial (like the US and UK) and metric systems agreed to define the 'international yard' as exactly 0.9144 meters. Since there are 36 inches in a yard, one inch was defined as 0.9144 / 36 = 0.0254 meters, which is exactly 2.54 centimeters.
