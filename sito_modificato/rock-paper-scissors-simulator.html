Data Source and Methodology

The simulator selects Rock, Paper, or Scissors at random with equal probability.

The Formula Explained

Outcomes follow the rules: rock beats scissors, paper beats rock, scissors beats paper. Identical choices are ties.

Glossary of Terms

  • Rock: A closed fist.
  • Paper: An open hand.
  • Scissors: A fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V.
