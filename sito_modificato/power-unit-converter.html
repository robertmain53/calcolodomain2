The Formula Explained

Conversions are based on the following formulas:

Watts to Horsepower: \( \text{HP} = \text{Watts} \times 0.00134102209 \)

Horsepower to Watts: \( \text{Watts} = \text{HP} \times 745.7 \)

BTU/hr to Watts: \( \text{Watts} = \text{BTU/hr} \times 0.29307107 \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Watt: A unit of power in the International System of Units (SI).
  • Horsepower: A unit of power that describes the rate at which work is done.
  • BTU/hr: British Thermal Unit per hour, a unit of power used in the power, steam generation, and heating industries.
