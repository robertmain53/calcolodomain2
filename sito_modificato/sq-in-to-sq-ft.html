Formulas for converting between square inches and square feet

Square inches (sq in) and square feet (sq ft) are both units of area in the imperial system. They are related through the basic length relationship between inches and feet.

Since \[ 1\ \text{ft} = 12\ \text{in} \] we have:

\[ 1\ \text{sq ft} = 1\ \text{ft} \times 1\ \text{ft} = 12\ \text{in} \times 12\ \text{in} = 144\ \text{sq in} \]

From square inches to square feet

To convert from square inches to square feet, divide by 144:

\[ \text{sq ft} = \frac{\text{sq in}}{144} \]

From square feet to square inches

To convert from square feet to square inches, multiply by 144:

\[ \text{sq in} = \text{sq ft} \times 144 \]

Worked examples

  • Example 1 – 432 sq in to sq ft
    \[ \text{sq ft} = \frac{432}{144} = 3\ \text{sq ft} \]
  • Example 2 – 7.5 sq ft to sq in
    \[ \text{sq in} = 7.5 \times 144 = 1{,}080\ \text{sq in} \]
  • Example 3 – 900 sq in to sq ft
    \[ \text{sq ft} = \frac{900}{144} \approx 6.25\ \text{sq ft} \]

How to use square inches and square feet in real projects

Manufacturers often give product dimensions in inches (e.g. tile size, board width, countertop depth), while room sizes and total area estimates are given in square feet. This converter helps you bridge that gap.

  • Flooring and tiling: work out how many tiles of a given size you need to cover a room in sq ft.
  • Countertops and furniture: convert detailed inch-based drawings to square feet for pricing.
  • Real estate: verify that described room sizes match floor plans or measurements.
  • DIY projects: estimate paint, varnish, or material coverage from small pieces to whole surfaces.

FAQ: Square inches to square feet

Why do we divide by 144 when converting sq in to sq ft?

Because area scales with the square of the length conversion. There are 12 inches in a foot, so the area ratio is \(12^2 = 144\). That means 1 square foot contains 144 square inches.

How do I calculate area in square inches from length and width?

First measure length and width in inches, then multiply:

\[ \text{area in sq in} = \text{length in in} \times \text{width in in} \] You can then convert to square feet by dividing the result by 144.

Can I convert directly from inches to square feet?

You cannot convert a length (inches) directly to an area (square feet). You must first have an area expressed in square inches (for example, by multiplying two lengths in inches), and then you can convert that area to square feet using the formulas above.
