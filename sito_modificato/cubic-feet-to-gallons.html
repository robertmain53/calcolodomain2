The Formula Explained

Gallons = Cubic Feet × 7.48052

Glossary of Terms

  • Cubic Feet: A unit of volume in the Imperial and U.S. customary measurement systems.
  • Gallons: A unit of volume for liquid measure equal to four quarts, in particular.
