The Formula Explained

Gallons = Cubic Feet × 7.48052

Glossary of Terms

  • Cubic Feet: A unit of volume in the Imperial and U.S. customary measurement systems.
  • Gallons: A unit of volume for liquid measure equal to four quarts, in particular.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For instance, if you have 2 cubic feet of water, to convert to gallons, multiply 2 by 7.48052 to get 14.96104 gallons.
