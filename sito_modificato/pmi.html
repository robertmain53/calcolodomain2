The Formula Explained

PMI = (Loan Amount × PMI Rate) / 12

Glossary of Variables

  • Home Price: The total price of the property.
  • Down Payment: The upfront payment made when purchasing the property.
  • Loan Term: The duration over which the loan is repaid.
  • Interest Rate: The annual rate charged by the lender.
  • PMI Rate: The percentage rate for mortgage insurance.

Come Funziona: Un Esempio Passo-Passo

Supponiamo che il prezzo della casa sia $300,000 con un acconto di $60,000. Il termine del prestito è di 30 anni con un tasso di interesse del 3.5% e un tasso PMI dello 0.5%. Il calcolo PMI mensile sarà: PMI = ((300,000 - 60,000) × 0.005) / 12 = $100 .

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is PMI?

PMI is insurance that protects the lender in case you default on your loan, required if your down payment is less than 20%.

How is PMI calculated?

PMI is calculated based on the loan amount and the PMI rate provided by the lender.

Can PMI be removed?

Yes, once you've accumulated enough equity in your home, you can request PMI cancellation.

Is PMI tax-deductible?

In some cases, PMI is tax-deductible. Consult a tax advisor for more information.

What affects the PMI rate?

The PMI rate is influenced by factors such as loan-to-value ratio and your credit score.
