Glossary of Terms

  • Age: The age of the individual taking the test.
  • Gender: The gender of the individual, as it may affect the fitness level calculation.
  • Heart Rate: The number of heartbeats per minute after completing the test.
  • Fitness Level: The calculated level of cardiovascular fitness.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the YMCA Step Test?
The YMCA Step Test is a simple cardiovascular endurance test using a 12-inch step.
How is the test performed?
Participants step up and down on a platform at a consistent rate for 3 minutes.
What is a good fitness level score?
A lower heart rate typically indicates better cardiovascular fitness.
Who can use this test?
This test is suitable for most individuals but consult a healthcare provider if unsure.
Is special equipment needed?
No special equipment is needed other than a step and a stopwatch.
