The Formula Explained

Time Dilation: \( \text{Destination Year} = \text{Departure Year} + \frac{\text{Travel Duration}}{\sqrt{1 - \left(\frac{\text{Travel Speed}}{c}\right)^2}} \)

Glossary of Variables

  • Travel Speed: Speed in terms of the speed of light (c).
  • Travel Duration: Duration of the journey in years.
