Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the Eurocode 7 standards. For more detailed information, refer to the official Eurocode 7 documentation.

The Formula Explained

\( Q = q_{b} \times A_{b} + q_{s} \times A_{s} \)

Glossary of Variables

  • Pile Length: The vertical length of the pile in meters.
  • Pile Diameter: The diameter of the pile in meters.
  • Soil Type: The type of soil where the pile is driven.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

To calculate the pile capacity, input the pile length, pile diameter, and select the soil type. The calculator uses the formula shown above to compute the capacity.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Eurocode 7?

Eurocode 7 is a European standard for geotechnical engineering design.
