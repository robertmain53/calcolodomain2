Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is dream interpretation?

Dream interpretation is the process of assigning meaning to dreams.

Can everyone interpret dreams?

While anyone can attempt to interpret dreams, understanding symbolism often requires experience or guidance.

Are interpretations scientifically proven?

Dream interpretations are subjective and not scientifically proven.

Can dreams predict the future?

While some believe dreams can predict future events, there is no scientific evidence supporting this claim.

Do all dreams have meanings?

Not all dreams have significant meanings; some may be random thoughts or reflections of the day.
