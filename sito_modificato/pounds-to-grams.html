Formula

\[ \text{Grams} = \text{Pounds} \times 453.59237 \]

Where 1 pound = 453.59237 grams (international avoirdupois pound).

How It Works

The international avoirdupois pound is legally defined as exactly 453.59237 grams. This conversion factor is recognized worldwide by both imperial and metric measurement systems.

Example Calculation

To convert 5 pounds to grams:

  1. Start with 5 pounds
  2. Multiply by 453.59237: 5 × 453.59237 = 2267.96185
  3. Result: 5 pounds = 2,267.96 grams

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert pounds to grams?

Multiply the number of pounds by 453.59237 to get the equivalent value in grams. For example, 2 pounds = 2 × 453.59237 = 907.18 grams.

What is a pound?

A pound (lb) is a unit of weight commonly used in the United States and other countries that use imperial measurements. One pound equals exactly 453.59237 grams.

Why is this conversion useful?

This conversion is essential when working with recipes from different countries, shipping packages internationally, or converting body weight between measurement systems.

How many grams are in a pound?

There are exactly 453.59237 grams in one pound. This is the internationally accepted conversion factor.
