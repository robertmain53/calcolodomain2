The Formula Explained

Glossary of Terms

  • Material Type: The type of material you are recycling (e.g., Aluminum, Plastic).
  • Amount: The quantity of material, measured in kilograms.
  • Estimated Savings: The potential monetary savings from recycling the specified material.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How accurate are the savings estimates?

The estimates are based on average recycling rates and market values. Actual savings may vary.

Can this calculator be used for commercial recycling?

Yes, the calculator is suitable for both individual and commercial recycling assessments.
