Steel Joist (K, LH, DLH) Design Calculator

This calculator is designed for construction professionals to easily determine the load capacity and specifications for K, LH, and DLH steel joists. It ensures precise structural design by adhering to industry standards.

Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are rigorously based on the Steel Joist Institute's specifications. For further details, visit the SJI website.

The Formula Explained

\[ \text{Capacity} = \frac{\text{Load} \times \text{Span Length}}{\text{Safety Factor}} \]

Glossary of Terms

  • Span Length: The distance between two supports.
  • Load: The weight distributed along the joist.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a steel joist?

A steel joist is a horizontal structural member used in framing to span an open space.

How do I choose the correct joist type?

Use this calculator to determine the appropriate joist based on load and span requirements.
