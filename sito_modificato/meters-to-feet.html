How to Convert Meters to Feet and Inches

Converting a measurement from meters (m) to feet (ft) and inches (in) is a common calculation for height, room dimensions, and more. It's a two-step process that requires first converting to total inches.

  1. Step 1: Convert Meters to Total Inches
    The international standard is that 1 inch is exactly 0.0254 meters. To convert, you divide your meter value by 0.0254 (or multiply by ~39.37).
    Formula: Total Inches = Meters / 0.0254
  2. Step 2: Convert Total Inches to Feet and Inches
    Divide the total inches by 12. The whole number is your **feet** part. The remainder is your **inches** part.

Example: Convert 1.80m to Feet and Inches

  • Step 1: 1.80 m / 0.0254 = 70.866... inches
  • Step 2: 70.866 / 12 = 5.9055.... The whole number is **5 feet**.
  • Step 3: 0.9055... × 12 = 10.866... inches.

Therefore, 1.80 meters is equal to **5' 10.87"** (5 feet and 10.87 inches).

Conversion Formulas

1 Foot (ft) = 0.3048 Meters (m) (Exactly)
1 Meter (m) = 3.28084 Feet (ft) (Approx.)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the formula for meters to feet?

The formula is: Feet = Meters × 3.28084. (or Meters / 0.3048).

How many feet are in a meter?

There are **3.28084 feet** in 1 meter.
