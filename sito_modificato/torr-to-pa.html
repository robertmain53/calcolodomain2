FAQ

What is Torr?

Torr is a unit of pressure based on an absolute scale, now defined as exactly 1/760 of a standard atmosphere.

How do I convert Torr to Pascals?

Use the formula: Pascals = Torr × 133.322.

Why use Pascals?

Pascals are the SI unit of pressure, commonly used in scientific and engineering contexts.
