How to use

  1. Enter the total area to tile and select the unit (sq ft or m²).
  2. Choose the trowel notch size used for installation.
  3. Add a waste factor (10–15% recommended).
  4. Enter bag size and unit to match your product.

Methodology

Area is normalized to square feet, waste is applied, and coverage is scaled for the chosen bag size. The final bag count is rounded up to ensure adequate material.
