Sales Tax Calculator (by State/Province)

This calculator helps you quickly determine the sales tax for any given amount based on U.S. state or Canadian province rates. Ideal for businesses and consumers who want to understand their total purchase costs.

The Formula Explained

To calculate sales tax, use the formula: Sales Tax = Amount × (Tax Rate / 100)

Glossary of Terms

  • Amount: The initial price before tax.
  • Tax Rate (%): The percentage of sales tax applied.
  • Sales Tax: The calculated tax amount.
  • Total Amount: The total cost including sales tax.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Sales Tax?

Sales tax is a tax paid to a governing body for the sales of certain goods and services.

How is Sales Tax Calculated?

Sales tax is calculated by applying a percentage rate to the taxable amount of a sale.
