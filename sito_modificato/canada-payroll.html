The Formula Explained

Net Income = Gross Income - (Federal Tax + Provincial Tax + CPP + EI)

Glossary of Terms

  • Gross Income: The total earnings before deductions.
  • Net Income: The amount of income left after deductions.
  • CPP: Canada Pension Plan contribution.
  • EI: Employment Insurance.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Canada Pension Plan (CPP)?

The CPP is a contributory, earnings-related social insurance program. It ensures a basic income for Canadian retirees and their families.

How often are payroll taxes updated?

Payroll taxes are typically reviewed and updated annually by the Canadian government.
