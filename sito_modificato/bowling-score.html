Glossary of Terms

  • Frame: A round in the game of bowling consisting of two rolls.
  • Strike: Knocking down all ten pins with the first roll.
  • Spare: Knocking down all the pins with two rolls.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I calculate my bowling score?

The score is calculated based on the pins knocked down in each frame, with bonuses for strikes and spares.

What is a perfect score in bowling?

A perfect game is a score of 300, which means you bowled 12 strikes in a row.
