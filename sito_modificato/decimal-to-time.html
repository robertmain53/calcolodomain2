The Formula Explained

The formula to convert decimal time to hours and minutes is:

Hours = floor(DecimalHours)

Minutes = (DecimalHours - Hours) × 60

Glossary of Terms

  • Decimal Hours: The time in decimal format, e.g., 2.75 hours.
  • Converted Time: The time converted into hours and minutes.
