The Formula Explained

Conversion Formula: ConvertedAmount = Amount × ExchangeRate

Glossary of Terms

  • Amount: The monetary value you wish to convert.
  • From Currency: The currency you are converting from.
  • To Currency: The currency you are converting to.
  • Exchange Rate: The rate at which one currency will be exchanged for another.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

Suppose you want to convert 100 USD to EUR. If the current exchange rate is 0.85, the converted amount will be calculated as: 100 USD × 0.85 = 85 EUR .
