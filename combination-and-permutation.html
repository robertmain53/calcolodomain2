The Formula Explained

Combination Formula: \( C(n, r) = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!} \)

Permutation Formula: \( P(n, r) = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!} \)

Glossary of Terms

  • n: Total number of items.
  • r: Number of items to select.
  • Combinations: Selection of items without considering the order.
  • Permutations: Arrangement of items where order matters.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the difference between combinations and permutations?

Combinations refer to selections of items without regard to the order, while permutations consider the order of selection.

How do I use this calculator?

Enter the total number of items (n) and the number of items to select (r), then click 'Calculate' to see the results.

Can I use this calculator for any value of n and r?

The values of n and r must be non-negative integers, and r should not exceed n.

Why are combinations and permutations important?

They are crucial in fields such as probability, statistics, and cryptography to determine possible outcomes and arrangements.

What if I enter invalid inputs?

The calculator will prompt you with an error message to correct your inputs.
