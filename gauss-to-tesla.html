Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Gauss?

Gauss is a unit of magnetic flux density corresponding to one maxwell per square centimeter.

What is Tesla?

Tesla is a unit of magnetic flux density equivalent to one weber per square meter.

Why convert Gauss to Tesla?

Conversion is necessary when working between systems that use different units of measurement for magnetic fields.
