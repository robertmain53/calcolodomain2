Glossary of Terms

Pound (lb)
A unit of mass used in the imperial system. The international avoirdupois pound is legally defined as exactly 453.59237 grams.
Gram (g)
A metric unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a kilogram. It is the base unit commonly used for measuring small to medium weights in the SI (International System of Units).
Avoirdupois System
A system of weights based on the pound of 16 ounces. It is the most common system for measuring weight in the United States.
