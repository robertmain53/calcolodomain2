Frequently Asked Questions

What is a growth percentile?

Percentiles compare your child’s height, weight, or BMI to children of the same sex and age. The 50th percentile is average.

Should I worry if my child's percentile is low or high?

A wide range is normal. Percentile changes over time matter more than a single number. Always consult your pediatrician about concerns.

Who should use length (recumbent) vs height (standing)?

Children under 2 years: measure length. Children 2 and older: use height.

Which standard should I use: CDC or WHO?

WHO is for 0–5 years worldwide; CDC for US children age 2–20. This tool provides broad estimates only.
