The Formula Explained

\( \text{Grams} = \text{Carats} \times 0.2 \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Carats: A unit of weight for precious stones and pearls, equivalent to 200 milligrams.
  • Grams: A metric unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a kilogram.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, to convert 5 carats to grams: \( 5 \times 0.2 = 1 \) gram.
