The Formula Explained

Glossary of Terms

  • Feet: A unit of linear measure equal to 12 inches (0.3048 m).
  • Meters: The fundamental unit of length in the metric system, equal to 100 centimeters or approximately 39.37 inches.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, to convert 10 feet to meters, multiply 10 by 0.3048 to get 3.048 meters.
