Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the standard conversion formula: 1 point = 1/72 inch. For more details, refer to the source.

The Formula Explained

Pixel Value = Point Value × 1.3333

Glossary of Terms

  • Point (pt): A unit of length in typography, equal to 1/72 of an inch.
  • Pixel (px): The smallest unit of a digital image displayed on a screen.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, converting 12 points to pixels: 12 × 1.3333 = 16 pixels.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a point?

A point is a unit of length used in typography, equal to 1/72 of an inch.

How do I convert points to pixels?

To convert points to pixels, multiply the point value by 1.3333.

Why use points in design?

Points are traditionally used in print design and are useful for maintaining consistency in font sizes across different media.
