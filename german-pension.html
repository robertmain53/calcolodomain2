Formula & Methodology

Monthly Pension = (Pension Points × Current Point Value) / 12
  • Current Point Value (2024): €37.60 (set by German law, subject to change)
  • Each year with average salary ≈ 1 pension point
  • If you earn above/below average, your points are proportionally higher/lower

Worked Example

  • Pension value: €37.60

Frequently Asked Questions

What are pension points?

Each year, you earn up to 1 point for earning Germany’s average salary. Higher or lower income, more or fewer points.

How is the point value set?

The point value (€) is updated by the government—check here for the current value.

Why does my salary matter?

Your annual salary determines the points you earn. Pension = points × value.
