The Formula Explained

Simple interest:

$$ I = P \times r \times t $$

Number of payments (rounded to the nearest whole payment):

$$ n = f \times t $$

Per-payment amount:

$$ \text{Payment} = \dfrac{P + I}{n} $$

Per-payment breakdown (simple equal split):

$$ \Delta P = \dfrac{P}{n}, \quad \Delta I = \dfrac{I}{n} $$

Total repaid:

$$ \text{Total} = P + I $$
