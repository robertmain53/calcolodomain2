The Formula Explained

The capacity of a helical pile is calculated using the formula:

Capacity = π × (Helix Diameter/2)^2 × Soil Capacity

Glossary of Terms

  • Pile Diameter: The diameter of the pile shaft.
  • Helix Diameter: The diameter of the helical plate.
  • Soil Capacity: The bearing capacity of the soil in pounds per square foot (psf).

Frequent Questions

What is a helical pile?

A helical pile is a deep foundation solution used to secure new or repair existing foundations.

How is the capacity affected by soil type?

The capacity can vary significantly based on the soil's bearing capacity.

Can I use this calculator for all soil types?

Yes, but the accuracy depends on the input of correct soil capacity values.
