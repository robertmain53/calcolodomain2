The Formula Explained

The formula used is: \( \text{BTU} = \text{Calories} \times 0.00396567 \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Calorie: A unit of energy commonly used in nutrition.
  • BTU: British Thermal Unit, a measure of heat energy.

Come Funziona: Un Esempio Passo-Passo

For example, if you input 500 calories into the converter, it will calculate as follows: \( 500 \times 0.00396567 = 1.982835 \) BTU.
