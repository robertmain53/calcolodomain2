The Formula Explained

The conversion from angstroms to nanometers is straightforward: Nanometers = Angstroms × 0.1.

Glossary of Terms

  • Angstrom: A unit of length equal to 10^-10 meters.
  • Nanometer: A unit of length equal to 10^-9 meters.

Example Calculation

For example, if you have 100 angstroms, the conversion to nanometers is: 100 × 0.1 = 10 nanometers.
