Formula

The conversion from miles to kilometers uses the following formula:

\[ \text{km} = \text{miles} \times 1.60934 \]

This conversion factor is based on the international definition: 1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers.

Data Source and Methodology

Step-by-Step Example

Problem: Convert 5 miles to kilometers.

Step 1: Write down the formula

\[ \text{km} = \text{miles} \times 1.60934 \]

Step 2: Substitute the value

\[ \text{km} = 5 \times 1.60934 \]

Step 3: Calculate the result

\[ \text{km} = 8.0467 \text{ km} \]

Answer: 5 miles = 8.05 kilometers
