The Formula Explained

The Penn State Equation is used to estimate the resting energy expenditure (REE) of critically ill patients.

\[ REE = 0.85 \times (10 \times weight + 6.25 \times height - 5 \times age + 5) \]

Glossary of Terms

  • Patient Weight: The current weight of the patient in kilograms.
  • Height: The height of the patient in centimeters.
  • Age: The age of the patient in years.
  • Caloric Needs: The estimated daily caloric needs of the patient.
