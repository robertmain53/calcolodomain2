How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, to convert 1000 grams to kilograms:

  1. Enter 1000 in the "Enter Value" field.
  2. Select "Grams" as the "From Unit".
  3. Select "Kilograms" as the "To Unit".
  4. Click "Convert" to see the result: 1 kilogram.
