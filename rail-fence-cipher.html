The Formula Explained

The Rail Fence Cipher works by writing your message diagonally over a set number of "rails".

Glossary of Terms

  • Message: The text you wish to encode or decode.
  • Number of Rails: The number of lines used in the cipher.

Come Funziona: Un Esempio Passo-Passo

To encode "HELLO" with 3 rails, write it diagonally over 3 lines and then read off each line in sequence.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Rail Fence Cipher?

The Rail Fence Cipher is a form of transposition cipher that gets its name from the way in which encryption is performed.

How do I use the Rail Fence Cipher?

To use the Rail Fence Cipher, input your message and specify the number of rails.

Is the Rail Fence Cipher secure?

While interesting, the Rail Fence Cipher is not secure by modern standards.

Can I decode a message with the Rail Fence Cipher?

Yes, by using the same number of rails used for encoding, you can decode a message.

What are some applications of the Rail Fence Cipher?

It is mainly used for educational purposes and simple encoding tasks.
