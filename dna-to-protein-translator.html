Glossary of Terms

  • DNA Sequence: A string of nucleotides that encodes genetic information.
  • Protein Sequence: The sequence of amino acids resulting from the translation of a DNA sequence.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is DNA Translation?

DNA translation is the process by which a protein is synthesized from the mRNA template derived from DNA.

Why is this tool important?

This tool helps to quickly convert DNA sequences to protein sequences, facilitating research and study in genetics.
