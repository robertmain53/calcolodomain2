Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate how much wallpaper I need?

Use the calculator by entering the dimensions of your walls and the wallpaper roll specifications.

What is the standard size of a wallpaper roll?

The standard wallpaper roll is typically 10 meters long and 0.52 meters wide.

Can I calculate wallpaper for multiple walls at once?

Currently, the calculator is designed for single wall calculations. Sum up the dimensions of your walls for a total estimate.

How accurate is the wallpaper calculator?

The calculator provides an estimate based on entered dimensions and roll specifications. Consider over-purchasing a roll for pattern matching and errors.
