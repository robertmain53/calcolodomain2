Glossary of Terms

  • Pascal (Pa): The SI unit of pressure.
  • Bar: A metric unit of pressure.
  • Atmosphere (atm): A unit of pressure based on average atmospheric pressure at sea level.
  • Psi: Pounds per square inch, a unit of pressure.

Example Calculation

To convert 1 atm to psi: 1 atm = 14.696 psi
