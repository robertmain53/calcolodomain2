Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a real option?

A real option is the right, but not the obligation, to undertake certain business initiatives, such as deferring, abandoning, expanding, or contracting a capital investment project.

How is real options valuation different from traditional valuation?

Real options valuation recognizes the value of managerial flexibility and strategic decision-making in response to unexpected market changes, unlike traditional valuation methods.
