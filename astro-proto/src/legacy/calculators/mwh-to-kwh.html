Formula to convert MWh to kWh

Megawatt hours and kilowatt hours are both units of energy. They differ only by a factor of 1,000:

1 megawatt (MW) = 1,000 kilowatts (kW)

1 megawatt hour (MWh) = 1,000 kilowatt hours (kWh)

Conversion formula:

\[ \text{kWh} = \text{MWh} \times 1{,}000 \]

\[ \text{MWh} = \frac{\text{kWh}}{1{,}000} \]

Worked examples

Example 1 – Convert 2.5 MWh to kWh

  1. Start with the value in MWh: 2.5 MWh
  2. Multiply by 1,000: \[ 2.5 \times 1{,}000 = 2{,}500 \]
  3. Result: 2.5 MWh = 2,500 kWh

Example 2 – Convert 7,500 kWh to MWh

  1. Start with the value in kWh: 7,500 kWh
  2. Divide by 1,000: \[ 7{,}500 \div 1{,}000 = 7.5 \]
  3. Result: 7,500 kWh = 7.5 MWh

What are MWh and kWh used for?

kWh (kilowatt hours) are most common on:

  • Household electricity bills
  • Appliance energy labels
  • Small solar and battery systems

MWh (megawatt hours) are used for larger scales, such as:

  • Utility-scale solar and wind farms
  • Data centers and industrial plants
  • Grid-scale battery storage
  • National or regional energy statistics

Relating MWh and kWh to power (kW, MW)

Both kWh and MWh measure energy, which is power over time:

  • Power is measured in kW or MW (rate of using energy).
  • Energy is measured in kWh or MWh (total used over time).

For example:

  • A 1 kW device running for 1 hour uses 1 kWh.
  • A 1 MW generator running for 1 hour produces 1 MWh (1,000 kWh).

FAQ: MWh to kWh

How do you convert MWh to kWh?

Multiply by 1,000. For example, 4.7 MWh × 1,000 = 4,700 kWh.

How do you convert kWh to MWh?

Divide by 1,000. For example, 18,000 kWh ÷ 1,000 = 18 MWh.

Why do utilities sometimes use MWh instead of kWh?

For large customers or system-level reporting, using kWh would lead to very large numbers. MWh keeps the figures more readable and easier to compare across plants, regions, or years.

Is 1 MWh a lot of energy?

Yes. As a rough guide, 1 MWh (1,000 kWh) is similar to the total electricity an average household might use in about a month, depending on location and lifestyle.
