The Formula Explained

The formula used for conversion is:

Megabytes = Kilobytes / 1024

Glossary of Terms

  • Kilobytes (KB): A unit of digital information equal to 1024 bytes.
  • Megabytes (MB): A unit of digital information equal to 1024 Kilobytes.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I convert Kilobytes to Megabytes?

Divide the number of Kilobytes by 1024 to get the equivalent Megabytes.

What is the formula for converting Kilobytes to Megabytes?

The formula is: Megabytes = Kilobytes / 1024.
