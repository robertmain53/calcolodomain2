The Formula Explained

For calculating the design load capacity:
Capacity = Width × Height × Material Strength

Glossary of Terms

  • Column Height: The vertical dimension of the column.
  • Column Width: The horizontal dimension of the column.
  • Material Strength: The compressive strength of the masonry material.
