Data Source and Methodology

All calculations are based on the standard conversion formula from hectares to acres, where 1 hectare equals 2.471 acres.

The Formula Explained

Glossary of Terms

  • Hectare: A metric unit of area defined as 10,000 square meters.
  • Acre: A unit of area commonly used in the United States and the UK, equivalent to 4,840 square yards.
