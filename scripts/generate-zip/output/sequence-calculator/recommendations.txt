### Topic: Sequence Calculator (`sequence-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student studying pre-calculus or discrete math, needing to find terms or sums of arithmetic or geometric sequences.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Many tools are single-purpose. A user has to go to different pages for arithmetic vs. geometric sequences.

**Recommendations for `sequence-calculator.html`:**
*   **Dual-Mode:** Allow the user to select "Arithmetic" or "Geometric."
*   **Comprehensive Inputs:** For arithmetic: first term, common difference, number of terms. For geometric: first term, common ratio, number of terms.
*   **Multiple Outputs:** Calculate and display: the nth term, the full sequence up to n, and the sum of the first n terms (the series).
*   **Formula Display:** For each calculation, show the relevant formula (`a_n = a_1 + (n-1)d` or `S_n = a_1 * (1-r^n)/(1-r)`) and the substituted values. This is critical for learning.
