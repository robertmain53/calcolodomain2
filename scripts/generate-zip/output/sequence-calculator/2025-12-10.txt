Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "sequence calculator"

Current Landscape: Sequence calculators (Arithmetic/Geometric helpers) usually output a list of terms and maybe the nth term using recursive formulas, but they seldom explain convergence, difference equations, or visualize how the sequence behaves as n grows.

To Surpass:
- Precision: When providing nth terms, indicate whether the expression is exact (in radicals) or approximated, show the recurrence used, and allow toggling between integer formatting and scientific notation for large values.
- Feature: Provide a “sequence explorer” that plots the terms, highlights ratios/differences, and optionally shows the generating function or closed form to help users compare arithmetic, geometric, or custom sequences quickly.
- Nuance: Offer a short discussion on how sequences appear in modeling (population growth, signal sampling), mention divergence vs convergence, and link to resources on recurrence relations so curious readers can dig deeper.
