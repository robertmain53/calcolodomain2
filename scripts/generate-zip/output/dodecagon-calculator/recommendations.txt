### Topic: Dodecagon Calculator (`dodecagon-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A geometry enthusiast or student working with regular polygons.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** A simple area/perimeter calculator. The value is in explaining the formula.

**Recommendations for `dodecagon-calculator.html`:**
*   **Side Length Input:** User enters the side length of a regular dodecagon (12-sided polygon).
*   **Area and Perimeter:** Calculate the area and perimeter.
*   **Explain the Area Formula:** The area formula for a regular dodecagon is complex. Break it down by showing that the dodecagon can be constructed from 12 isosceles triangles and explain how the formula is derived from the trigonometry of those triangles. A visual diagram is essential here.
