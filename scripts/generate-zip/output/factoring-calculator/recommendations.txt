### Topic: Factoring Calculator (`factoring-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** An algebra student who needs to factor polynomials.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Many factoring tools are available, but they often just give the final answer without showing any intermediate steps.

**Recommendations for `factoring-calculator.html`:**
*   **Handles Different Types:** The calculator should be able to factor trinomials (`x^2 + bx + c`), difference of squares (`a^2 - b^2`), and polynomials with a greatest common factor (GCF).
*   **Step-by-Step Factoring:** For a trinomial like `x^2 + 5x + 6`, it should show the process: "We need two numbers that multiply to 6 and add to 5. These numbers are 2 and 3. Therefore, the factored form is (x+2)(x+3)."
*   **Link to Roots:** After factoring, state the roots of the polynomial. For `(x+2)(x+3)`, the roots are -2 and -3. This connects factoring to solving equations.
