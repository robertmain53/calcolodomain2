Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "cone volume calculator"

Current Landscape: Cone volume helpers compute (1/3)πr²h but rarely clarify whether the height is slant or vertical, whether the base is included, or how rounding interacts with manufacturing tolerances.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Spell out the formula, highlight that h is the perpendicular height, allow unit toggles, and show how decimal precision carries through to volume so engineers can quote the exact value.
- Feature: Provide a 3D sketch where the user drags radius and height, show volume/area updates in real time, and optionally stack multiple cones to visualize cumulative volumes.
- Nuance: Include short notes on applications (storage silos, lamp shades, aerodynamic nose cones) and link to derivations for the curious reader, giving insight beyond the quick number.
