### Topic: Cone Volume Calculator (`cone-volume-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student or professional needing to find the volume of a cone.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The calculation is simple. The best way to add value is to compare it to a cylinder.

**Recommendations for `cone-volume-calculator.html`:**
*   **Inputs and Formula:** Inputs for radius (r) and height (h). Display the formula `V = (1/3) * π * r^2 * h`.
*   **Cylinder Comparison:** This is the key feature. After calculating the cone's volume, also calculate the volume of a cylinder with the *same radius and height*.
*   **Visual and Textual Explanation:** State clearly: "The volume of the cone is exactly 1/3 the volume of a cylinder with the same base and height." Show a visual with a cone fitting inside a cylinder to make this relationship unforgettable.
