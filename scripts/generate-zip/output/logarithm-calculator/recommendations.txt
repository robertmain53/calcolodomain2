### Topic: Logarithm Calculator (`logarithm-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student or scientist who needs to calculate a logarithm with a specific base.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Simple log calculators are common, but they often fail to explain the underlying relationship between logarithms and exponents.

**Recommendations for `logarithm-calculator.html`:**
*   **Flexible Input:** Allow the user to calculate `log_b(x)` by providing inputs for both the base `b` and the number `x`.
*   **Explain the Answer:** When the calculator shows `log_2(8) = 3`, it should also display the equivalent exponential form: `2^3 = 8`. This reinforces the core concept.
*   **Change of Base Formula:** Include a section explaining the change of base formula (`log_b(x) = log_c(x) / log_c(b)`), showing users how they could calculate any log using a standard scientific calculator.
*   **Common Logarithms:** Have quick buttons for common bases like base 10 (log), base e (ln), and base 2 (lb).
