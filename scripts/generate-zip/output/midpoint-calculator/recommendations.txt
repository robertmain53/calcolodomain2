### Topic: Midpoint Calculator (`midpoint-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A geometry student needing to find the center point between two other points.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** A simple formula, but the concept is visual.

**Recommendations for `midpoint-calculator.html`:**
*   **Coordinate Input:** Simple inputs for (x1, y1) and (x2, y2).
*   **Show the Formula:** Display the midpoint formula `M = ((x1+x2)/2, (y1+y2)/2)` and substitute the user's values, showing the calculation for each coordinate separately.
*   **Visual Plot:** Plot the two endpoints and then plot the calculated midpoint, showing that it is visually in the center of the line segment connecting them.
