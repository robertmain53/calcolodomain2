Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "percentage calculator"

Current Landscape: Percentage tools compute simple ratios (part/whole ×100) but typically ignore contexts like percent change, compounding, or measurement uncertainty propagation.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Clearly state the operation (relative change, share of total), allow selection between percent increase/decrease, markup vs markdown, and keep the decimals accurate for reporting.
- Feature: Provide scenario cards (concentration, efficiency, finance) that let users plug values and instantly see results plus the formulas, enabling both quick answers and narrative context.
- Nuance: Mention the theoretical basis (percent as a fraction over 100), discuss when to treat percentages as rates vs simple ratios, and link to resources on uncertainty propagation so curious readers can explore further.
