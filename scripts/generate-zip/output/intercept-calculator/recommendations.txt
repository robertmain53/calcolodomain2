### Topic: Intercept Calculator (`intercept-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** An algebra student learning to graph linear equations.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Finding intercepts is straightforward, but the concept is deeply visual. Most calculators ignore this.

**Recommendations for `intercept-calculator.html`:**
*   **Equation Input:** Allow users to enter a linear equation in various forms (`y = mx + b`, `Ax + By = C`).
*   **Step-by-Step Solution:** To find the y-intercept, show the process of setting `x=0` and solving for `y`. To find the x-intercept, show the process of setting `y=0` and solving for `x`.
*   **Interactive Graph:** Graph the line and clearly label the x-intercept and y-intercept points on the axes. This provides immediate visual feedback and reinforces the concept that intercepts are where the line crosses the axes.
