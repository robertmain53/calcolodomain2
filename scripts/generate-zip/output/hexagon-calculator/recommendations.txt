### Topic: Hexagon Calculator (`hexagon-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A geometry student, board gamer, or someone working with tiled patterns.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The area calculation is often presented as a complex formula without explanation.

**Recommendations for `hexagon-calculator.html`:**
*   **Side Length Input:** Input for the side length (s) of a regular hexagon.
*   **Breakdown into Triangles:** The best way to teach the area formula is to show that a regular hexagon is composed of **six equilateral triangles**.
*   **Step-by-Step Area:** First, calculate the area of one of the equilateral triangles (using `(s^2 * sqrt(3))/4`). Then, multiply that result by 6 to find the total area of the hexagon. This makes the formula intuitive rather than something to be memorized.
*   **Diagram:** Show the hexagon with the six component triangles drawn inside.
