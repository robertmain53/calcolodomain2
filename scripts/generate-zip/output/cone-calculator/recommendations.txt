### Topic: Cone Calculator (`cone-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student or professional needing a full analysis of a cone's properties.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Often, volume and surface area are on separate pages. A comprehensive tool is better.

**Recommendations for `cone-calculator.html`:**
*   **Unified Tool:** This should be a single destination for all cone calculations.
*   **Inputs:** Radius (r) and height (h).
*   **Calculated Properties:** Calculate and display the Volume, Slant Height, Lateral Surface Area, Base Area, and Total Surface Area.
*   **Show Formulas:** Display the formula used for each calculation next to the result to provide maximum educational value. An interactive 3D model is also highly recommended.
