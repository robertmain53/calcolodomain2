### Topic: Axis of Symmetry Calculator (`axis-of-symmetry-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** An algebra student working with parabolas.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Simple calculation, but lacks the crucial visual component.

**Recommendations for `axis-of-symmetry-calculator.html`:**
*   **Parabola Input:** Allow user to input the coefficients of a quadratic equation `ax^2 + bx + c`.
*   **Formula and Calculation:** Show the formula `x = -b / 2a`, substitute the values, and calculate the result.
*   **Interactive Graph:** Graph the parabola and draw the vertical line representing the axis of symmetry. Also, plot the vertex, as its x-coordinate is the axis of symmetry. This visual link is the most important part for understanding.
