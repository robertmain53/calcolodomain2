Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "tangent line calculator"

Current Landscape: Calculators for tangent lines often produce slope and equation from a point or curve, but they rarely explain which derivative they used, fail to mention precision near vertical tangents, and offer no visualization of curve versus tangent.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Clarify whether the tangent was derived analytically (derivative) or numerically, show the slope to desired decimal places, and note any domain restrictions so readers can trust the tangent on steep regions.
- Feature: Plot the function and overlay the tangent line at the chosen point, display the differential change (Δy vs Δx), and include a toggle to show secant lines converging for intuitive derivation.
- Nuance: Add a short explanation of why tangents matter (optics, instantaneous rates) and links to calculus references so readers can refresh the theory while still getting the quick line equation.
