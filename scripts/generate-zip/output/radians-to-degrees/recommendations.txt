### 9. Topic: Radians to Degrees (`radians-to-degrees`)

**Critique and Recommendations:** Essential for trigonometry and physics. Existing calculators are simple. To be the best, the page must visually explain *what a radian is*—the angle subtended when the arc length equals the radius. An animated GIF or interactive diagram showing a radius wrapping around a circle's circumference would be invaluable for deep understanding. It should then derive the conversion factor from the fact that 2π radians = 360°.
