### 7. Topic: Inches to cm (`inches-to-cm`)

**Critique and Recommendations:** Similar to other unit conversions, this is a simple calculation (1 inch = 2.54 cm). To surpass others, the page should explain that this is an *exact* definition from the 1959 agreement, not an approximation. Visual aids comparing an inch and 2.54 cm on a ruler would be highly effective.
