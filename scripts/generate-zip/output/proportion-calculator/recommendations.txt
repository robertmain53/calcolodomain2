### Topic: Proportion Calculator (`proportion-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student learning about ratios, or anyone needing to solve a proportion (e.g., for scaling a recipe or a map).

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The calculation is simple cross-multiplication, but the interface can be confusing.

**Recommendations for `proportion-calculator.html`:**
*   **Intuitive Interface:** Present the proportion as `A/B = C/D` with three input boxes and one for the unknown (e.g., 'x'). The user fills in any three to solve for the fourth.
*   **Show the Work:** Explicitly show the cross-multiplication step: `A * D = B * C`, then show the final division to solve for the unknown variable.
*   **Real-World Examples:** Provide a tab with common word problems that can be solved with proportions, such as "If 3 apples cost $2, how much do 10 apples cost?" and show how to set up the problem in the calculator.
