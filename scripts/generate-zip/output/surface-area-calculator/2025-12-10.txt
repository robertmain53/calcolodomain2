Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "surface area calculator"

Current Landscape: Surface area calculators offer formulas for common solids but often don't clarify whether lateral surfaces include bases, or how units convert and precision ties into coatings/paint estimates.

To Surpass:
- Precision: List each solid’s formula, show whether caps are included, provide unit toggles, and allow rounding control to keep the output consistent with finish tolerances.
- Feature: Provide a visual for each solid, annotate the contributing faces, and supplement with a “coating estimator” that translates surface area into paint/thermal coating volume for practical projects.
- Nuance: Mention where surface area matters (heat transfer systems, material science), highlight how texture can change effective area, and link to geometric resources so readers can explore the theoretical foundations after the quick number.
