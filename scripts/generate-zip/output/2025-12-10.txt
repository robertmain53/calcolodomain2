Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Objective: Define requirements for the definitive calculation/conversion resource.

1. Keyword: "mm to inches" (mm-to-inches)
   Current Landscape: Most tools provide a simple multiplication factor (0.03937). They lack mechanical context.
   To Surpass:
   - Precision: Must use the exact definition of 1 inch = 25.4 mm (1959 International Yard and Pound).
   - Feature: Implement a "Machinist Mode" that converts decimal mm to fractional inches (e.g., 1/16, 1/32, 1/64) with error tolerance.
   - Nuance: Include a dynamic SVG ruler showing the physical scale comparison on screen (calibrated to user's PPI).

2. Keyword: "capital gains tax" (capital-gains-tax)
   Current Landscape: Generic calculators that miss local tax nuances or specific deduction rules.
   To Surpass:
   - Precision: Real-time integration of current year tax brackets (Federal + State for US context, or Country selector).
   - Feature: "Scenario Modeling" allowing users to input purchase price, sale price, and duration to see the cliff between Short-term and Long-term rates.
   - Nuance: Explain the "Wash Sale" rule and Net Investment Income Tax (NIIT) triggers with interactive tooltips.

3. Keyword: "exponent calculator" (exponent-calculator)
   Current Landscape: Basic input fields. Fails on large numbers or complex bases.
   To Surpass:
   - Precision: Arbitrary-precision arithmetic to handle results exceeding standard floating-point limits.
   - Feature: Support for negative bases, fractional exponents, and complex numbers (i).
   - Nuance: Visualize the exponential curve (y=x^n) and step-by-step derivation using logarithm properties for solving variables.

4. Keyword: "typography converter" (typography-converter)
   Current Landscape: Static tables. Confusing relationship between physical (pt) and digital (px, em) units.
   To Surpass:
   - Precision: Allow user to define the root font size (default 16px) for accurate em/rem conversion.
   - Feature: Live text preview pane showing the font rendering changes in real-time.
   - Nuance: Deep dive into the history of the "point" (DTP point vs American point) and why 72pt = 1 inch in digital typography.

5. Keyword: "kg to lbs" (kg-to-lbs)
   Current Landscape: Simple multipliers.
   To Surpass:
   - Precision: Use the exact value 1 lb = 0.45359237 kg.
   - Feature: "Mass vs Weight" toggle explaining the physics (Newtons) vs colloquial usage.
   - Nuance: Include "Stones" for UK audiences and a "Grocery Mode" for estimating food quantities.

6. Keyword: "cm to inches" (cm-to-inches)
   Current Landscape: Redundant with mm-to-inches but often used for height or screen size.
   To Surpass:
   - Precision: High decimal accuracy.
   - Feature: "Height Converter" preset that outputs Feet + Inches (e.g., 5' 10") alongside decimal inches.
   - Nuance: Visual comparison of common objects (e.g., credit card width, A4 paper).

7. Keyword: "lbs to kg" (lbs-to-kg)
   Current Landscape: Standard inversion of kg-to-lbs.
   To Surpass:
   - Precision: Exact inverse calculation.
   - Feature: Bulk conversion table generator for gym weights (plates) or luggage limits.
   - Nuance: Discussion on the kilogram redefinition (Planck constant) vs the artifact prototype.

8. Keyword: "btu to kwh" (btu-to-kwh)
   Current Landscape: Confusing energy unit conversion without time context.
   To Surpass:
   - Precision: Distinguish between BTU (IT) and BTU (th).
   - Feature: "Appliance Context" - input AC tonnage or heater rating to see energy consumption over time.
   - Nuance: Explain Power (kW) vs Energy (kWh) vs Heat Content (BTU) thermodynamics.

9. Keyword: "angstrom to nm" (angstrom-to-nm)
   Current Landscape: Very niche, often poor UI.
   To Surpass:
   - Precision: Exact 10:1 ratio, but focus on scientific notation handling.
   - Feature: "Atomic Scale" visualization showing bond lengths or atom radii.
   - Nuance: History of Anders Jonas Ångström and why the unit persists in crystallography despite SI preference for nm.

10. Keyword: "kelvin to fahrenheit" (kelvin-to-fahrenheit)
    Current Landscape: Abstract formulas.
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: Exact conversion F = 1.8(K - 273.15) + 32.
    - Feature: Interactive thermometer slider covering Absolute Zero to Planck Temperature.
    - Nuance: Explain the thermodynamic necessity of Kelvin and the historical definition of Fahrenheit points (brine, body heat).

11. Keyword: "degrees to radians" (degrees-to-radians)
    Current Landscape: Simple math, often lacks geometric intuition.
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: Output in terms of Pi (e.g., 2Pi, Pi/2) rather than just decimals.
    - Feature: Interactive Unit Circle where dragging the angle updates both values.
    - Nuance: Animation showing the radius wrapping around the circumference to define the radian.

12. Keyword: "cups to tablespoons" (cups-to-tablespoons)
    Current Landscape: Chaos due to regional standards (US, Metric, Imperial).
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: Must allow selection of "US Customary", "US Legal", "Imperial", and "Metric" cup.
    - Feature: "Recipe Scaler" allowing input of fractions (e.g., 1/3 cup).
    - Nuance: Visual stacking of tablespoons to fill a cup.

13. Keyword: "barrels to gallons" (barrels-to-gallons)
    Current Landscape: Ambiguous "barrel".
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: Strict separation of Oil Barrel (42 gal), Beer Barrel (31 gal), and Dry Barrel.
    - Feature: "Industry Selector" (Petroleum vs Agriculture vs Brewing).
    - Nuance: Historical context of the 42-gallon oil barrel standard (Blue Barrel).

14. Keyword: "meters to nm" (meters-to-nm)
    Current Landscape: Large powers of 10.
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: Scientific notation support (10^9).
    - Feature: "Zoom" visualization from human scale (m) down to light wavelengths (nm).
    - Nuance: Electromagnetic spectrum chart mapping wavelengths to colors/types of radiation.

15. Keyword: "tablespoons to teaspoons" (tablespoons-to-teaspoons)
    Current Landscape: Simple 3:1 ratio (US).
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: Handle Australian tablespoons (20ml) vs US/UK (15ml).
    - Feature: Kitchen math helper for halving/doubling recipes.
    - Nuance: Include "Dessertspoon" and archaic measurements like "Saltspoon".

16. Keyword: "micrometers to nm" (micrometers-to-nm)
    Current Landscape: Simple 1000x factor.
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: Focus on biological scales.
    - Feature: Comparative size tool: Red blood cell vs Bacteria vs Virus vs DNA width.
    - Nuance: Discussion on manufacturing tolerances (semiconductors) where these units matter most.

17. Keyword: "pascals to psi" (pascals-to-psi)
    Current Landscape: Engineering conversion, often dry.
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: High decimal precision for scientific applications.
    - Feature: "Pressure Gauge" visual showing atmospheric, tire, and hydraulic pressure contexts.
    - Nuance: Explain Gauge Pressure (psig) vs Absolute Pressure (psia) and the Pascal as a derived SI unit.

18. Keyword: "fahrenheit to kelvin" (fahrenheit-to-kelvin)
    Current Landscape: Reverse of #10.
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: Exact formula inversion.
    - Feature: Cryogenics focus (Liquid Nitrogen, Helium points).
    - Nuance: Why Kelvin is used in gas laws (PV=nRT) and Fahrenheit is not.

19. Keyword: "ounces to grams" (ounces-to-grams)
    Current Landscape: Confusion between weight and volume.
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: 1 oz = 28.3495 g.
    - Feature: Explicit toggle for "Fluid Ounces" (Volume) vs "Ounces" (Mass) vs "Troy Ounces" (Precious Metals).
    - Nuance: Food packaging context (Nutrition label rounding rules).

20. Keyword: "feet to meters" (feet-to-meters)
    Current Landscape: Standard length.
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: Differentiate International Foot (0.3048 m) vs US Survey Foot (1200/3937 m).
    - Feature: Altitude converter (Aviation context: Flight Levels).
    - Nuance: The history of the Mendenhall Order.

21. Keyword: "km to miles" (km-to-miles)
    Current Landscape: Travel distance.
    To Surpass:
    - Precision: 1 mile = 1.609344 km.
    - Feature: "Pace Converter" (min/km to min/mile) for runners.
    - Nuance: Map visualization showing the distance radius on a local map.
