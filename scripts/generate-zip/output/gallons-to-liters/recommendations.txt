### Topic: Gallons to Liters (`gallons-to-liters`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A traveler, a car owner comparing fuel efficiency, or a homebrewer. They need a quick conversion but might be confused by the difference between US gallons and Imperial gallons.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Most converters are functional but often don't specify which gallon (US or Imperial) they are using by default, leading to potential inaccuracies.

**Recommendations for `gallons-to-liters.html`:**
*   **Prominent Unit Selection:** The calculator should have a clear toggle or dropdown for "US Gallon" vs. "Imperial Gallon" right at the top.
*   **Historical Note:** Briefly explain the historical split between the US and Imperial systems, noting that the US gallon is based on the older English wine gallon.
*   **Volume Visualization:** Show a visual comparison of a US gallon, an Imperial gallon, and a liter to help users understand the size differences.
*   **Contextual Examples:** Provide examples for different use cases, like fuel economy (MPG vs. L/100km) and beverage sizes.
