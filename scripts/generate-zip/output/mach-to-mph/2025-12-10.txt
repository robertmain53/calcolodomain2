Keyword: "mach to mph"

Current Landscape: Converters apply Mach × speed of sound but usually fix the speed of sound at 767 mph; they rarely allow you to adjust for altitude/temperature or call out the dependence on air properties.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Multiply the Mach number by the actual local speed of sound (customizable via temperature or ISA), keep decimal precision for high-speed events, and spell out the formula used so the result can be cited accurately.
- Feature: Show a chart mapping Mach to mph for different altitudes, display equivalent kph and knots, and include a quick explanation of why Mach 1 varies with conditions.
