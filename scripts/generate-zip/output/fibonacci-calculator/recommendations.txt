### Topic: Fibonacci Calculator (`fibonacci-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student, programmer, or math enthusiast curious about the Fibonacci sequence.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Most calculators just give the nth Fibonacci number. They don't explain the sequence or its interesting properties.

**Recommendations for `fibonacci-calculator.html`:**
*   **Nth Number and Sequence:** Allow a user to find the nth number, but also display the entire sequence up to that point.
*   **Golden Ratio Connection:** As the sequence gets larger, calculate the ratio of adjacent terms (F(n)/F(n-1)) and show how it converges to the Golden Ratio (φ ≈ 1.618).
*   **Recursive Definition:** Clearly state the definition: `F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2)`.
*   **Nature and Art:** Include a small, engaging section with images showing where the Fibonacci sequence appears in nature (shells, flower petals, pinecones).
