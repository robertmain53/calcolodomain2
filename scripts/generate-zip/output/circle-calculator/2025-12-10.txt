Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "circle calculator"

Current Landscape: Circle calculators (UnitConverters, MathIsFun, RapidTables) return radius/diameter/area/perimeter but seldom document which inputs drive which formula or surface the interplay between area and circumference for tolerance work.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Always show the concrete formulas (A=πr², C=2πr) with the π precision used and allow toggling between exact symbolic output and decimal results for instrumentation specs.
- Feature: Display an interactive circle where the user drags radius/diameter and sees both area and circumference update, including a slider for unit conversions (mm/cm/in) and circumference-to-area ratio.
- Nuance: Add a short discussion on why circle constants matter in waveguides and mechanical systems, plus links to proofs (Archimedes, Gauss) so readers can refresh the theory behind the quick computation.
