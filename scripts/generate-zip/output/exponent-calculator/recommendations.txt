### Topic: Exponent Calculator (`exponent-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** Anyone needing to calculate `x` raised to the power of `y`.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** A very basic operation. To be superior, a page must explain the rules of exponents.

**Recommendations for `exponent-calculator.html`:**
*   **Base and Exponent Input:** Simple inputs for base (`x`) and exponent (`y`).
*   **Handle Special Cases:** Correctly handle negative exponents (`x^-y = 1/x^y`), fractional exponents (`x^(1/y)` is the y-th root of x), and a zero exponent (`x^0 = 1`).
*   **Exponent Rules Section:** Include a dynamic educational section. When the user performs a calculation, show a related rule. For example, if they calculate `2^-3`, highlight the "Negative Exponent Rule". Have a reference list of all key rules: Product Rule, Quotient Rule, Power of a Power Rule, etc.
