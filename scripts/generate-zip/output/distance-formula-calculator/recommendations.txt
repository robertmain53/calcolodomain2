### Topic: Distance Formula Calculator (`distance-formula-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A geometry student who needs to find the distance between two points in a Cartesian plane.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** These calculators provide the answer but don't connect the formula to its geometric origin, the Pythagorean theorem.

**Recommendations for `distance-formula-calculator.html`:**
*   **Coordinate Input:** Simple inputs for (x1, y1) and (x2, y2).
*   **Visual Plot:** Plot the two points on a graph. Draw the line segment between them, and also draw the horizontal and vertical components to form a right triangle.
*   **Show the Formula:** Display the distance formula `d = sqrt((x2-x1)^2 + (y2-y1)^2)` and substitute the user's values into it, showing the step-by-step calculation. Explicitly label the `(x2-x1)` part as the triangle's base and `(y2-y1)` as its height, connecting it directly to `a^2 + b^2 = c^2`.
