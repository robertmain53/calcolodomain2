### Topic: Angle Calculator (`angle-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student learning the basics of geometry and angles.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** "Angle calculator" is generic. A good page would focus on the relationships between angles.

**Recommendations for `angle-calculator.html`:**
*   **Focus on Relationships:** Instead of a generic tool, make this a calculator for finding complementary and supplementary angles.
*   **Interactive Input:** A user enters an angle. The calculator instantly provides its complement (90° - angle) and its supplement (180° - angle).
*   **Visual Diagrams:** For a given angle, show a diagram of it forming a right angle with its complement, and another diagram of it forming a straight line with its supplement. This makes the concepts instantly clear.
