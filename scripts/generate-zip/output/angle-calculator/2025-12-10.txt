Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "angle calculator"

Current Landscape: Angle calculators convert between degrees/radians, compute supplementary/complementary values, but often lack discussion of quadrant, wrap-around, or precision when used for direction-sensitive scientific instruments.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Show the chosen units, mention wrap-around logic (mod 360 or 2π), display the result to configurable precision, and indicate whether the angle is normalized for navigation or math contexts.
- Feature: Provide a circular dial visualization, highlight the angle, let users flip between unit systems, and optionally compute related vectors or slopes to tie the number back to physics or instrumentation.
- Nuance: Offer a short note on quadrant selection, mention how negative angles are interpreted, and link to Euler’s circle or orientation references so the curious reader can explore the theory beyond the quick conversion.
