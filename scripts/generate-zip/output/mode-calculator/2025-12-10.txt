Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "mode calculator"

Current Landscape: Quick stats helpers (CalculatorSoup, OmniCalculator, Statology) give you the mode by listing the most frequent value(s) but seldom check for multimodal sets, show frequency tables, or tie the statistic to practical interpretations.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Compute the mode plus all co-modes, report their frequencies and relative frequencies, and expose whether the dataset is unimodal, bimodal or multimodal so the user can trust the claim.
- Feature: Provide a “distribution view” with a histogram or dot plot, letting readers adjust bins and instantly see how the mode shifts, plus allow grouping by class intervals when dealing with continuous data.
- Nuance: Offer a short note on when mode is more informative than mean/median (modal analysis in materials, most common measurement), and link to further resources about frequency behaviour so readers can explore the theoretical background.
