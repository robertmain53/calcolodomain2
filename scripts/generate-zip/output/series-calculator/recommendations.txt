### Topic: Series Calculator (`series-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A pre-calculus or calculus student needing to find the sum of an arithmetic or geometric series.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Often confused with "sequence calculators." A series calculator is specifically for the *sum*.

**Recommendations for `series-calculator.html`:**
*   **Clear Distinction:** This is the same core functionality as the "Sequence Calculator" but should be framed differently. The primary output is the **sum**.
*   **Formula-Driven:** The page should be built around the summation formulas (`S_n`). When a user enters the parameters of a sequence (e.g., first term, common difference, number of terms), the main result should be the sum of that series.
*   **Show the Summation:** Display the formula, substitute the values, and show the final sum. Also, list the terms of the sequence being added together for clarity (e.g., `Sum = 2 + 4 + 6 + 8 = 20`).
*   **Infinite Series:** For geometric series, include a section on infinite series. If the common ratio `|r| < 1`, calculate the sum to infinity using the formula `S = a_1 / (1-r)`.
