### Topic: Chord Calculator (`chord-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A geometry student or machinist working with circles.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The relationship between a chord, its angle, and the circle's radius can be confusing. Visuals are essential.

**Recommendations for `chord-calculator.html`:**
*   **Interactive Diagram:** Show a circle with a radius that can be adjusted. Allow the user to define a chord by either its length, the central angle it subtends, or its distance from the center. The diagram should update in real-time.
*   **Calculate All Properties:** Based on the user's two inputs (e.g., radius and chord length), calculate all other related properties: central angle, arc length, and the area of the circular segment created by the chord.
*   **Show the Trigonometry:** Use diagrams to show the right triangle formed by the radius, half the chord length, and the line from the center to the chord. This reveals the underlying trigonometry used in the calculations.
