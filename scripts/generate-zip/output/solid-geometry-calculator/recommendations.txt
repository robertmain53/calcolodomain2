### Topic: Solid Geometry Calculator (`solid-geometry-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A geometry student who needs to calculate volume and surface area for various 3D shapes.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Individual shape calculators are common, but a unified "solid geometry" tool is rare.

**Recommendations for `solid-geometry-calculator.html`:**
*   **Shape Hub:** This page should be a visual menu of common 3D shapes (Cube, Sphere, Cylinder, Cone, Pyramid, Rectangular Prism).
*   **Interactive Models:** For each shape, show an interactive 3D model that the user can rotate. As they input dimensions (radius, height, etc.), the model should update.
*   **Dual Calculation:** For each shape, calculate and display both the Volume and the Surface Area, along with the formulas used for each.
