Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "solid geometry calculator"

Current Landscape: Solid geometry tools typically offer volume/surface area formulas for spheres, cylinders, prisms but seldom tie the results to construction tolerances, units, or visualization of cross-sections.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Show the exact formula used (including π precision, slant height) and allow the user to choose units, so engineers trust the result for CAD and specification sheets.
- Feature: Add a “build preview” that plots the solid with interactive sliders for dimensions, shows cross-sectional diagrams, and overlays the volume/surface calculations, making the quick answer tangible.
- Nuance: Include a short note contrasting right vs oblique solids, mention how manufacturing tolerances ripple through volume, and link to theoretical references about Euler’s formula or geometric invariants for readers who want to dive deeper.
