### Topic: Decimal to Fraction (`decimal-to-fraction`)

**User Persona & Goal:** Anyone who has a decimal and wants to express it as a fraction.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The conversion is standard. A superior page would also handle repeating decimals.

**Recommendations for `decimal-to-fraction.html`:**
*   **Standard Conversion:** For a decimal like 0.75, show the steps: `0.75 = 75/100`, then show the simplified fraction `3/4`.
*   **Repeating Decimal Conversion:** This is a key differentiator. Implement the algorithm to convert repeating decimals (e.g., 0.333... -> 1/3, or 0.1666... -> 1/6). This requires more complex logic but provides a much more powerful tool.
*   **Mixed Numbers:** For decimals greater than 1 (e.g., 2.25), provide the answer as both an improper fraction (9/4) and a mixed number (2 1/4).
