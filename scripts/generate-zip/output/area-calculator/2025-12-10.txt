Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "area calculator"

Current Landscape: General area calculators present a dropdown for common shapes but rarely explain which formula they use, how units convert, or provide a reference when a shape is not regular.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Make each shape’s formula explicit, show units for both inputs and outputs, and allow toggling decimal precision with SI conversions so engineers can cite the result precisely.
- Feature: Provide a “shape lab” where you pick a shape (circle, polygon, composite) and see the formula derive dynamically with annotated diagrams and intermediate steps.
- Nuance: Include notes on composite area decomposition, mention numerical integration for irregular shapes, and link to further resources so readers can explore theory beyond quick sums.
