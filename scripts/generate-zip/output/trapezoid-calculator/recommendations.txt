### Topic: Trapezoid Calculator (`trapezoid-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A geometry student needing to find the area and perimeter of a trapezoid.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The formulas are simple, but visualizing the components (bases, height, legs) is key.

**Recommendations for `trapezoid-calculator.html`:**
*   **Visual Diagram:** Have a clear, labeled diagram of a trapezoid, showing `base a`, `base b`, `height h`, and the two legs `c` and `d`.
*   **Area and Perimeter:** Calculate both. For Area, show the formula `A = ((a+b)/2) * h`. For Perimeter, show `P = a + b + c + d`.
*   **Median Calculation:** Also calculate the median of the trapezoid (the line segment connecting the midpoints of the legs) and show its formula `m = (a+b)/2`. Explain that the area can also be thought of as `median * height`.
