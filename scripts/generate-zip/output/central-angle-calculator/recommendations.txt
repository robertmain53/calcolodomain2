### Topic: Central Angle Calculator (`central-angle-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A geometry student working with circles and arcs.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The relationship between arc length, radius, and central angle is key.

**Recommendations for `central-angle-calculator.html`:**
*   **Solve for Any Variable:** The formula is `Arc Length = Radius * Central Angle (in radians)`. The calculator should allow the user to input any two of these values and solve for the third.
*   **Degrees and Radians:** The calculator must be able to accept the angle in degrees or radians and provide the result in both. This reinforces the importance of radians in geometric formulas.
*   **Interactive Diagram:** Show a circle where the user can visually adjust the radius and arc length (or angle) and see the other values change in response.
