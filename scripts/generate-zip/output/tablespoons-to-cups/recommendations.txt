### 8. Topic: Tablespoons to Cups (`tablespoons-to-cups`)

**Critique and Recommendations:** A crucial conversion for cooking. Most sites give the answer (16 tbsp = 1 cup in the US). A superior page would address regional differences (e.g., US vs. UK vs. Australian tablespoons have slightly different volumes) and provide a comprehensive conversion chart including teaspoons, fluid ounces, and milliliters to be a one-stop cooking conversion resource.
