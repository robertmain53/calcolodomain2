### Topic: Decimal Calculator (`decimal-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student or professional needing to perform arithmetic with decimals, possibly with a specified number of significant figures or rounding.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** A standard calculator. To add value, it needs to focus on the specific properties of decimal arithmetic.

**Recommendations for `decimal-calculator.html`:**
*   **Standard Operations:** Perform addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
*   **Rounding Control:** Allow the user to specify the number of decimal places to round the result to.
*   **Repeating Decimals:** If a result is a repeating decimal (e.g., 1/3 = 0.333...), display it with proper notation (a bar over the repeating part) in addition to the rounded decimal.
*   **Link to Fraction Converter:** Since many decimal calculations result in numbers that are cleaner as fractions, provide a prominent link to a "Decimal to Fraction" converter.
