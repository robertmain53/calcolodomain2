### Topic: Variance Calculator (`variance-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A statistics student learning about measures of data dispersion.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The distinction between sample variance and population variance is often poorly explained or missing entirely.

**Recommendations for `variance-calculator.html`:**
*   **Sample vs. Population:** The most important feature is a toggle for the user to select whether their data is a "Sample" or a "Population."
*   **Show the Formula:** Display the correct formula based on the user's selection (dividing by `n-1` for sample, `n` for population).
*   **Step-by-Step Table:** This is crucial for learning. Show a table with columns: `Data Point (x)`, `Mean (μ)`, `(x - μ)`, and `(x - μ)^2`. Then show the sum of the last column and the final division to get the variance.
*   **Standard Deviation:** Since standard deviation is just the square root of the variance, calculate and display it as well. Explain that it's often more intuitive because it's in the same units as the original data.
