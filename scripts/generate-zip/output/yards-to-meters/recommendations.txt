### 1. Topic: Yards to Meters (`yards-to-meters`)

**User Persona & Goal:** An enthusiast-scientist needing a quick conversion but also interested in the historical and official definitions of these units.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Most online converters offer a simple input for yards and an output in meters. While fast, they lack context. They don't explain the historical evolution of the yard or the meter, nor do they detail the international agreement that standardized the conversion factor (1 yard = 0.9144 meters exactly).

**Recommendations for `yards-to-meters.html`:**
*   **Interactive Converter:** A clean, prominent calculator for immediate conversion.
*   **Historical Context:** A section on the history of the Imperial yard and the French origin of the metric system.
*   **The Official Standard:** Explain the International Yard and Pound Agreement of 1959, which standardized the conversion. This builds trust and provides a definitive source.
*   **Beyond Length:** Briefly touch upon how this fundamental conversion impacts other measurements like area (square yards to square meters) and volume.
*   **Practical Examples:** Use real-world examples, such as converting the length of a football field or a standard running track.
