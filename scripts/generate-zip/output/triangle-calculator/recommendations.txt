### Topic: Triangle Calculator (`triangle-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A geometry or trigonometry student needing to solve a triangle (find all sides and angles).

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Many calculators require specific inputs (e.g., only SSS or SAS). A truly great one is more flexible.

**Recommendations for `triangle-calculator.html`:**
*   **Flexible Input:** Allow the user to input any three known properties (e.g., Side-Side-Side, Side-Angle-Side, Angle-Side-Angle, etc.), as long as they form a valid triangle.
*   **Show the Laws:** Based on the inputs, show the application of the Law of Sines and/or the Law of Cosines to solve for the unknown parts.
*   **Area Calculation:** Once the triangle is solved, calculate its area (using Heron's formula for SSS or `1/2 ab sin(C)` for SAS).
*   **Visual Representation:** Draw the solved triangle to scale.
