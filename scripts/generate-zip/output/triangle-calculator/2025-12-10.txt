Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "triangle calculator"

Current Landscape: Triangle calculators (Heron-based) compute area given sides/angles, but they rarely highlight which law they used, how rounding affects angles, or which triangle type (right/isosceles) is assumed.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Show the law or formula used (Heron, sine rule, etc.), display intermediate steps, and keep the angle precision adjustable so readers working with surveying or structural data can rely on the numbers.
- Feature: Provide an interactive triangle sketch where you can drag sides/angles, automatically update the height, medians, and area, and offer a “checks” panel verifying triangle inequality and orientation.
- Nuance: Include short explanations of triangle centers (centroid, circumcenter) and mention how triangle geometry ties into trigonometric identities so curious scientists can explore the deeper theory after their quick calculation.
