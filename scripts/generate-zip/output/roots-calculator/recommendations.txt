### Topic: Roots Calculator (`roots-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** An algebra student trying to find the roots (zeros) of a polynomial.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Most calculators solve for roots but don't explain the methods used or handle complex roots well.

**Recommendations for `roots-calculator.html`:**
*   **Polynomial Input:** A simple interface to enter the coefficients of a polynomial (e.g., for `ax^2 + bx + c`).
*   **Show the Method:** For quadratic equations, display the solution using the quadratic formula. For higher-order polynomials, mention the method used (e.g., Rational Root Theorem, numerical methods like Newton-Raphson).
*   **Complex and Real Roots:** Clearly list both real and complex roots.
*   **Graphical Representation:** Plot the polynomial and mark the locations where it crosses the x-axis (the real roots). This provides invaluable visual confirmation.
