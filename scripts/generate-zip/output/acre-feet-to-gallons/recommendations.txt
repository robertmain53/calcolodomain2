### Topic: Acre-Feet to Gallons (`acre-feet-to-gallons`)

**User Persona & Goal:** An individual in agriculture, water resource management, or civil engineering dealing with large-scale water volumes.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** This is a niche conversion. Existing pages are very basic, offering a number and little else. They miss the opportunity to explain the real-world significance.

**Recommendations for `acre-feet-to-gallons.html`:**
*   **Clear Definition:** Start by defining an acre-foot: the volume of water required to cover one acre of land to a depth of one foot.
*   **Relatable Visualizations:** Provide powerful visualizations for the resulting number of gallons. For example: "X acre-feet is enough water to supply Y households for a year" or "equivalent to Z Olympic-sized swimming pools."
*   **Application Focus:** Include sections on how this unit is used in reservoir capacity, irrigation water allocation, and flood control planning.
