Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "range calculator"

Current Landscape: Most range calculators (RapidTables, OmniCalculator, SohoDistance, etc.) just subtract min from max, add a small FAQ, and skip the interpretative layers about outliers, data cleaning, or how range behaves with measurement noise.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Show the full min/max pair, the computed range, and optional trimming (interquartile range or trimmed range) with toggles, so readers understand the sensitivity to extremes.
- Feature: Add a “data explorer” panel that plots the dataset, lets you remove suspected outliers, and dynamically recalculates the range while annotating the values that define it.
- Nuance: Include a short note on when range is meaningful (instrument span vs distribution width) and when other dispersion metrics (variance, MAD) are preferable, with links for users who want to dive deeper.
