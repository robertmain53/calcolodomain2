### Topic: Range Calculator (`range-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A statistics student learning about measures of spread.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** A very simple calculation (Max - Min). Existing tools offer the answer but no context.

**Recommendations for `range-calculator.html`:**
*   **Clear Calculation:** After the user inputs a data set, show the result by explicitly identifying the maximum and minimum values and displaying the subtraction: `Range = [Max Value] - [Min Value]`.
*   **Visual Data Plot:** Display the numbers on a number line to visually represent the spread of the data and the location of the min and max.
*   **Beyond the Range:** Briefly explain why the range can sometimes be a misleading measure of spread (e.g., it's sensitive to outliers) and introduce more robust measures like the Interquartile Range (IQR). Offer a link to an IQR calculator.
