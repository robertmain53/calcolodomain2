### 2. Topic: Square Meters to Square Feet (`square-meters-to-square-feet`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A hobbyist architect or DIY enthusiast planning a project who needs to convert between metric and imperial area units. They need precision for materials and costing.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Standard converters are plentiful. However, they rarely provide visual aids to help users grasp the difference in scale. They also fail to address common pitfalls, like confusing linear feet with square feet.

**Recommendations for `square-meters-to-square-feet.html`:**
*   **Visual Comparison Tool:** An interactive graphic that shows a square meter and a square foot overlaid, allowing the user to visualize the ~10.764x difference.
*   **Underlying Formula:** Clearly show that the conversion factor (1 m² ≈ 10.764 ft²) is derived from the linear conversion (1 m ≈ 3.28084 ft) squared. This clarifies the "why."
*   **Project-Based Estimator:** Include a feature where users can input room dimensions (in either unit) and get the area in both, along with a warning about using area units, not linear ones.
*   **Real-World Application:** Discuss common uses, such as real estate listings (which often use both), flooring, and landscaping calculations.
