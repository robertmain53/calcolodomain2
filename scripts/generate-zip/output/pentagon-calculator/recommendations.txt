### Topic: Pentagon Calculator (`pentagon-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A geometry student or hobbyist interested in the properties of a regular pentagon.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Most just calculate area and perimeter. They miss the interesting connections to the Golden Ratio.

**Recommendations for `pentagon-calculator.html`:**
*   **Side Length Input:** The main input should be the side length of a regular pentagon.
*   **Core Calculations:** Calculate the perimeter, area, and interior/exterior angles.
*   **Diagonal and Golden Ratio:** Calculate the length of a diagonal. Then, show that the ratio of the diagonal to the side length is the Golden Ratio (φ ≈ 1.618). This is a fascinating property that most calculators ignore and adds significant educational value.
*   **Labeled Diagram:** A clear diagram showing the side, apothem, radius, and a diagonal.
