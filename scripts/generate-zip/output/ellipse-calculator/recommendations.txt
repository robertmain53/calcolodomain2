### Topic: Ellipse Calculator (`ellipse-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A pre-calculus or physics student studying conic sections or orbits.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The area formula is simple, but the circumference is notoriously difficult. Most calculators only give a rough approximation.

**Recommendations for `ellipse-calculator.html`:**
*   **Inputs and Diagram:** Inputs for semi-major axis (a) and semi-minor axis (b) with a clear diagram.
*   **Area:** Calculate the exact area with the formula `A = πab`.
*   **Circumference (Perimeter):** This is the key. Explain that there is no simple exact formula. Provide the result from one or more of Ramanujan's approximations, which are very accurate. This shows a deeper level of knowledge than competitors.
*   **Other Properties:** Also calculate and explain other properties like the foci and eccentricity. Graph the ellipse and mark the foci.
