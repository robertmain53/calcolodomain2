### Topic: Coordinate Calculator (`coordinate-calccalculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student or hobbyist working with geometry, needing to perform various calculations related to coordinates.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The name is generic. A superior page would need to be a hub for various coordinate geometry calculations.

**Recommendations for `coordinate-calccalculator.html`:**
*   **Consolidate Functionality:** This page should act as a portal. Instead of being a single calculator, it should offer a menu of coordinate-based calculations: Distance Formula, Midpoint Formula, Slope of a Line, Equation of a Line from two points.
*   **Unified Graph:** Have a single interactive graph where users can plot points and then select an operation to perform on them. The results (e.g., the midpoint, the connecting line) should appear on the same graph.
*   **Clear Explanations:** Each sub-calculator should link to a detailed explanation of the formula it uses, similar to the recommendations for the individual calculator pages.
