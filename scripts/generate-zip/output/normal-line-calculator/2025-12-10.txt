Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "normal line calculator"

Current Landscape: Normal line calculators compute the slope of a perpendicular from a point to a given line but often stop at the numeric value without explaining the geometric construction, projection length, or how the result enters optimization problems.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Provide the slope/angle pair for the normal, show the foot of the perpendicular, and allow the user to control rounding so the quick number is ready for reporting.
- Feature: Visualize the original line, the point, and the normal segment, display the distance along the normal, and optionally compute the reflection of the point across the line for deeper investigation.
- Nuance: Mention where normals are used (least squares, surface normals, optics), link to the inner product-based derivation, and offer references so curious readers can explore the theoretical significance beyond the quick geometry.
