### Topic: Normal Line Calculator (`normal-line-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A calculus student who has learned to find the tangent line and is now learning about the normal line.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Finding the normal line is a niche calculus topic. Most calculators focus only on the tangent.

**Recommendations for `normal-line-calculator.html`:**
*   **Function and Point Input:** User inputs a function `f(x)` and a point `x`.
*   **Step-by-Step Calculus:**
    1.  Show the derivative of the function, `f'(x)`.
    2.  Calculate the slope of the **tangent** line at the point, `m_tan = f'(x)`.
    3.  Calculate the slope of the **normal** line by showing the formula `m_norm = -1 / m_tan`. Explain that the normal line is perpendicular to the tangent line.
    4.  Use the point-slope form to find the equation of the normal line.
*   **Graph Both Lines:** The best feature would be to graph the original function, the tangent line at the point, and the normal line at the point, showing the perpendicular relationship visually.
