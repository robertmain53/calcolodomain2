### Topic: Right Triangle Calculator (`right-triangle-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student in geometry or trigonometry focusing specifically on right triangles.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Many exist, but a superior one would integrate all aspects of right-triangle math.

**Recommendations for `right-triangle-calculator.html`:**
*   **Input Any Two:** Allow the user to input any two properties (two sides, or a side and an angle).
*   **Pythagorean Theorem:** If two sides are known, show the Pythagorean theorem (`a^2 + b^2 = c^2`) being used to find the third side.
*   **SOHCAHTOA:** If a side and angle are known, show the trigonometric ratios (sin, cos, tan) being used to find the other sides. Define SOHCAHTOA clearly.
*   **All Properties:** Calculate and display all three sides, all three angles (including the 90° one), the area, and the perimeter.
