Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "right triangle calculator"

Current Landscape: Right triangle tools give side lengths/angles via trig but seldom contextualize which side is the hypotenuse, how rounding errors creep in at small angles, or how to surface both exact and approximate results.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Label the hypotenuse, legs, and angles clearly, display the exact sine/cosine ratios, and keep decimal precision consistent with measurement tolerance so the output can be trusted in construction or optics.
- Feature: Provide a dynamic triangle diagram with draggable points, automatically compute missing sides/angles, area, altitude, and display the Pythagorean check so engineers can verify consistency at a glance.
- Nuance: Include a short explanation of when right triangles model physical systems (projectile motion, stress analysis) and link to references on trigonometric identities so curious readers can deepen their theoretical understanding.
