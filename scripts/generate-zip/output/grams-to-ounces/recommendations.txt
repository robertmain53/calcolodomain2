### Topic: Grams to Ounces (`grams-to-ounces`)

**User Persona & Goal:** Someone cooking, mailing a package, or dealing with precious metals. The context of the conversion is critical.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Most converters don't distinguish between the standard Avoirdupois ounce (for general weight) and the Troy ounce (for precious metals). This is a significant source of error.

**Recommendations for `grams-to-ounces.html`:**
*   **Dual-Mode Calculator:** The primary feature should be a converter that lets the user choose between "Avoirdupois Ounce" (≈28.35g) and "Troy Ounce" (≈31.1g).
*   **Contextual Explanation:** Clearly explain when to use each type of ounce. Avoirdupois is for food, postage, and body weight. Troy is for gold, silver, and gemstones.
*   **Historical Background:** Briefly touch on the origins of these two different weight systems to provide deeper understanding and build user trust.
