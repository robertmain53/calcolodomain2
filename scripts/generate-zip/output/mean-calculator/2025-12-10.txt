Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "mean calculator"

Current Landscape: Many calculators simply return the arithmetic mean with minimal context, rarely clarifying if the data is weighted, trimmed, or contains missing values.

To Surpass:
- Precision: State the formula used, display the sorted dataset with weights if provided, and allow the user to toggle between arithmetic, geometric, or harmonic means so the chosen metric matches the scientific context.
- Feature: Add a “robust summary” view that compares the mean to median and mode, flags outliers, and optionally recomputes after trimming to illustrate sensitivity.
- Nuance: Provide a short note on when to prefer weighted means or geometric averages (e.g., growth rates), plus links to statistics literature so curious researchers can refresh the theory behind the quick number.
