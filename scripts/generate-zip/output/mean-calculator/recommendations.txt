### Topic: Mean Calculator (`mean-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student learning basic statistics.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The simplest measure of central tendency. The value-add is in showing the process.

**Recommendations for `mean-calculator.html`:**
*   **Show the Sum and Count:** After the user enters data, explicitly show the sum of all the numbers and the count of the numbers.
*   **Display the Calculation:** Write out the full calculation: `Mean = (Sum of Numbers) / (Count of Numbers) = [Sum] / [Count] = [Mean]`.
*   **Link to Median and Mode:** Since these are taught together, provide easy links to the Median and Mode calculators for a complete analysis of central tendency.
