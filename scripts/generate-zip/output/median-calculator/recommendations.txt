### Topic: Median Calculator (`median-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A statistics student learning about measures of central tendency.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The process of finding a median is more complex than for the mean or mode. Simply giving the answer misses the teaching opportunity.

**Recommendations for `median-calculator.html`:**
*   **Show the Sorting:** After the user enters a data set, the first thing the page should do is display the data set sorted in ascending order. This is the crucial first step.
*   **Odd vs. Even Count:** The logic for finding the median differs for odd vs. even-sized data sets. The calculator should detect this and explain the process.
    *   **Odd:** "The data set has [N] numbers (an odd count), so the median is the middle number: [Median]."
    *   **Even:** "The data set has [N] numbers (an even count), so the median is the average of the two middle numbers: ([A] + [B]) / 2 = [Median]."
*   **Visual Highlight:** In the sorted list, visually highlight the middle number(s) used to find the median.
