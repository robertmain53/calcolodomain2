### 3. Topic: Acres to Square Feet (`acres-to-square-feet`)

**User Persona & Goal:** Someone involved in land surveying, real estate, or agriculture. They need a reliable conversion and might be curious about the agrarian origin of the acre.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** The conversion is straightforward (1 acre = 43,560 sq ft). Most sites just state this fact. They miss the opportunity to explain the fascinating history of the acre.

**Recommendations for `acres-to-square-feet.html`:**
*   **Primary Converter:** A simple acre-to-square-feet calculator.
*   **Historical Definition:** Explain the origin of the acre as the amount of land tillable by one man with one ox in one day. Connect it to the "furlong" and "chain."
*   **Visualization:** Provide relatable comparisons, e.g., "An acre is about 90% of an American football field."
*   **Related Units:** Include a table converting acres to other units like hectares, square miles, and square kilometers to provide a broader context for land measurement.
