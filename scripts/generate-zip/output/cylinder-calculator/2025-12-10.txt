Analysis Report - 2025-12-10
Persona: Enthusiast-Scientist
Keyword: "cylinder calculator"

Current Landscape: Cylinder tools compute volume/surface area but rarely note whether the height includes caps or which units are assumed, making it hard to trust for tanks or flow calculations.

To Surpass:
- Precision: Spell out each formula (volume = πr²h, surface area excluding/including caps), expose π precision, and allow unit toggles so users can copy the exact number for engineering specs.
- Feature: Provide a schematic with adjustable radius/height, show the generated volume as liters or cubic meters, and overlay fill levels or material thickness so the results feel practical.
- Nuance: Discuss the role of cylinders in storage, hydraulics, and optics, and link to dimensional analysis resources, letting the curious user refresh the theoretical background while still getting the quick answer.
