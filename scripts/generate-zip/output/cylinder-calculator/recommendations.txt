### Topic: Cylinder Calculator (`cylinder-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** A student or DIYer needing to calculate the volume or surface area of a cylinder.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Basic functionality. A superior page would break down the surface area calculation.

**Recommendations for `cylinder-calculator.html`:**
*   **Interactive 3D Model:** A rotatable cylinder that updates with user inputs for radius (r) and height (h).
*   **Volume:** Calculate volume using `V = π * r^2 * h`.
*   **Surface Area Breakdown:** Calculate the total surface area, but also show its components: the area of the top circle (`πr^2`), the area of the bottom circle (`πr^2`), and the area of the side (`2πrh`). An animation showing the cylinder "unrolling" into two circles and a rectangle would be an exceptional teaching tool.
