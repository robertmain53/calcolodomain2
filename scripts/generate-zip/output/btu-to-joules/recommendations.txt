### 10. Topic: BTU to Joules (`btu-to-joules`)

**Critique and Recommendations:** An energy conversion common in HVAC and science. The key complexity is that there are multiple definitions of the British Thermal Unit (BTU). A top-tier page would have a converter but also explain the different BTU standards (e.g., ISO 31-4, Thermochemical) and which one is being used (typically ~1055 joules). It should also define both units in fundamental terms (BTU: energy to raise 1 lb of water by 1°F; Joule: work done by a force of one newton over one meter).
