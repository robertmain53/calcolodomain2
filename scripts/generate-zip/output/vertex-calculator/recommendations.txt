### Topic: Vertex Calculator (`vertex-calculator`)

**User Persona & Goal:** An algebra student studying parabolas and needing to find the vertex.

**Critique of Existing Pages:** Finding the vertex is a formulaic process, but its meaning is visual.

**Recommendations for `vertex-calculator.html`:**
*   **Multiple Input Forms:** Allow users to enter the parabola's equation in standard form (`ax^2+bx+c`) or vertex form (`a(x-h)^2+k`).
*   **Show the Calculation:** For standard form, show the calculation for the x-coordinate (`h = -b/2a`) and then the process of plugging `h` back into the equation to find the y-coordinate (`k`).
*   **Interactive Graph:** Graph the parabola and place a prominent, labeled point at the vertex. State whether the vertex is a minimum (if the parabola opens up) or a maximum (if it opens down).
