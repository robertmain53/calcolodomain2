The Formula Explained

Speed (mph) = Mach × 761.2

Glossary of Terms

  • Mach Number: A dimensionless unit representing the ratio of the speed of an object to the speed of sound.
  • Miles per Hour (mph): A unit of speed expressing the number of miles traveled in one hour.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Mach speed?

Mach speed refers to the speed of an object moving through air or any other fluid medium, compared to the speed of sound in that medium.

Why is the speed of sound used?

The speed of sound is a natural constant used as a reference point for high-speed travel, especially in aviation and aerospace.

Can Mach numbers be negative?

No, Mach numbers represent a ratio of speed, hence they are always positive or zero.

Does altitude affect Mach speed?

Yes, as altitude increases, the speed of sound decreases, thus affecting the Mach number.

What factors affect the speed of sound?

The speed of sound varies based on the medium's composition, temperature, and pressure.
