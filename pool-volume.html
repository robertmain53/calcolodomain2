The Formula Explained

The formula used for calculating pool volume varies depending on the shape:

  • Rectangular: Volume = Length × Width × Depth
  • Oval: Volume = Length × Width × Depth × 0.85
  • Circular: Volume = π × (Radius²) × Depth

Glossary of Variables

  • Length: The longest side of the pool.
  • Width: The shorter side of the pool.
  • Depth: The average depth of the pool.
  • Radius: The radius of a circular pool, calculated as half the diameter.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

Consider a rectangular pool with a length of 10 meters, a width of 5 meters, and an average depth of 2 meters. The volume is calculated as follows:

Volume = 10 × 5 × 2 = 100 m³
