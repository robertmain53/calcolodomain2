The Formula Explained

Atomic Weight Calculation: \( \text{Atomic Weight} = \sum (\text{isotopic mass} \times \text{abundance})\)

Glossary of Variables

  • Element Symbol: The one or two-letter abbreviation of an element.
  • Atomic Number: The number of protons in the nucleus of an atom.
  • Atomic Weight: The weighted average of the atomic masses of the element's isotopes.
