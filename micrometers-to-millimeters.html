The Formula Explained

Convert micrometers to millimeters using the formula:
\( \text{Millimeters} = \frac{\text{Micrometers}}{1000} \)

Glossary of Terms

  • Micrometer (μm): A unit of length equal to one millionth of a meter.
  • Millimeter (mm): A unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter.

Practical Example

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

For example, to convert 5000 micrometers to millimeters, divide 5000 by 1000 to get 5 millimeters.
