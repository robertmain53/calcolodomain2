Data Source and Methodology

The Formula Explained

Fuel Cost = (Distance / Fuel Efficiency) × Fuel Price

Glossary of Terms

  • Distance: The total miles you plan to drive.
  • Fuel Efficiency: Your vehicle's miles per gallon (mpg).
  • Fuel Price: The current cost per gallon of fuel.
