How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

  1. Convert 100 km/h to m/s: \( 100 \times \frac{1000}{3600} = 27.777\ldots \text{ m/s} \).

  3. Express in g: \( \frac{3.472}{9.80665} \approx 0.354 \text{ g} \).
