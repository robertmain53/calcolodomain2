Formula

ft/s = mph × 1.46667

1 mile per hour = 1.46667 feet per second. To convert, multiply (mph) × 1.46667.

How Does the Conversion Work?

There are 5280 feet in a mile and 3600 seconds in an hour.
So, 1 mph = 5280 ft ÷ 3600 s = 1.46667 ft/s

Example Calculation

  1. Convert 60 mph to ft/s
  2. 60 × 1.46667 = 88.0 ft/s
  3. Result: 60 mph = 88.0 ft/s

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert mph to feet per second?

Multiply the speed in mph by 1.46667. For example: 20 mph × 1.46667 = 29.33 ft/s.

Is mph faster than ft/s?

Yes. 1 mph is 1.46667 ft/s. Conversely, 1 ft/s is only about 0.68 mph.
