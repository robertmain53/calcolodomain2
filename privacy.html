1. Introduction

Welcome to CalcDomain. This Privacy Policy explains how Yeah Up S.r.l. ("we," "us," or "our"), operating CalcDomain.com, collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website and use our calculators.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This policy complies with:

  • The European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable data protection laws

Key Principle: CalcDomain operates on a privacy-first model. We do not store your calculation data, track your activity for advertising purposes, or sell your personal information to third parties. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us:

Contact Forms

When you contact us through our website, we collect:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Subject matter of inquiry
  • Message content
  • Optional: Calculator name (if inquiry relates to specific tool)

Newsletter Subscription (Optional)

If you opt to receive updates:

  • Email address
  • Subscription preferences
  • Date of subscription

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Basic Analytics (Non-Personal)

We collect minimal, aggregated analytics data to improve our service:

  • Page views (which calculators are most popular)
  • Geographic location (country/region level only, not precise location)
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Browser type and version
  • Referring website (how you found us)
  • Time spent on pages

Log Files

Our web server automatically records:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Date and time of visit
  • Pages accessed
  • HTTP response codes

Note: Log files are retained for 30 days for security and troubleshooting purposes, then automatically deleted.

2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

We explicitly DO NOT collect or store:

  • Calculator input data (income, debts, health metrics, etc.)
  • Calculation results
  • Social Security numbers or tax identification numbers
  • Credit card or financial account information
  • Medical records or health information
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Social media profiles or posts
  • Biometric data

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Where Your Data is Processed

Calculator computations occur entirely in your browser. We do not transmit, store, or process your calculator inputs or results on our servers.

Data you voluntarily provide (contact forms, newsletter subscriptions) is stored on secure servers within the European Union, in compliance with GDPR requirements.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server configurations with regular security updates
  • Access controls limiting who can view personal data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Automatic logout for admin sessions

4.3 Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary:

  • Contact Form Submissions: 2 years from last contact
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Until you unsubscribe
  • Log Files: 30 days
  • Analytics Data: 14 months (anonymized)

After retention periods expire, data is permanently deleted from our systems.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

5.1 Essential Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies required for website functionality:

  • Session cookies: Maintain your session while browsing (expires when you close browser)
  • Cookie consent: Remember your cookie preferences (expires after 12 months)

5.2 Analytics Cookies (Optional)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics with IP anonymization to understand how our site is used. You can opt out at any time.

5.3 What We DON'T Use

We do NOT use:

  • Advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Cross-site tracking
  • Fingerprinting technologies
  • Third-party advertising networks

5.4 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality. Instructions for popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you're in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., unsubscribe from emails)
  • Lodge Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

7.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know: What personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of sale of personal information (Note: We don't sell data)
  • Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy rights exercise

7.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

  • Email us at: privacy@calcdomain.com
  • Include "Privacy Rights Request" in the subject line
  • Provide sufficient detail for us to verify your identity
  • Specify which right(s) you wish to exercise

We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA) of receiving your request.

8. Children's Privacy

CalcDomain is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@calcdomain.com. We will promptly delete such information.

Our calculators are educational tools that can be used by all ages, but we do not collect personal information from users regardless of age.

9. International Data Transfers

CalcDomain is operated by Yeah Up S.r.l., an Italian company. Our servers are located in the European Union, ensuring compliance with GDPR standards.

If you access our website from outside the EU, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the EU. By using CalcDomain, you consent to this transfer.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission.
