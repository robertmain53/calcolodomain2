Data Source and Methodology

Data is derived from standard conversion rates commonly used in navigation and aviation. All calculations are based on the conversion factor: 1 knot = 1.15078 miles per hour and 1.852 kilometers per hour.

The Formula Explained

MPH Conversion: MPH = Knots × 1.15078

KPH Conversion: KPH = Knots × 1.852

Glossary of Terms

  • Knots: A unit of speed equal to one nautical mile per hour.
  • MPH: Miles per hour, a unit of speed expressing the number of miles traveled in one hour.
  • KPH: Kilometers per hour, a metric unit of speed expressing the number of kilometers traveled in one hour.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

If you input 10 knots, the conversion to MPH will be 10 × 1.15078 = 11.51 MPH, and to KPH will be 10 × 1.852 = 18.52 KPH.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a knot?

A knot is a unit of speed equal to one nautical mile per hour, commonly used in maritime and aviation contexts.

How do I convert knots to MPH?

To convert knots to miles per hour, multiply the speed in knots by 1.15078.

How do I convert knots to KPH?

To convert knots to kilometers per hour, multiply the speed in knots by 1.852.

Why is this conversion useful?

Converting knots to MPH or KPH is useful for understanding speed in different contexts, such as aviation and maritime navigation.
