Knots to MPH/KPH Converter

Convert knots to miles per hour (MPH) and kilometers per hour (KPH). Ideal for navigation, marine, aviation, and scientific speed conversions.

Formulas

MPH = Knots × 1.15078
KPH = Knots × 1.852

1 knot = 1.15078 mph and = 1.852 kph. Multiply knots by the appropriate factor.

Example Calculation

  • 10 knots × 1.15078 = 11.51 mph
  • 10 knots × 1.852 = 18.52 kph

Common Use Cases

  • Navigation: Boat or aircraft speed in knots can be quickly compared to mph or kph.
  • Weather and wind: Meteorological speeds, wind warnings, and forecasts.
  • Travel: Maritime, international flight briefings, and chart plotting.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you convert knots to mph and kph?

Multiply by 1.15078 for mph, and by 1.852 for kph.

Where is this conversion useful?

In navigation, weather forecasting, flight planning, and whenever speed data is in knots but you need familiar values (mph/kph).
