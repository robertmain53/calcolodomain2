Formula

The conversion from kilometers to miles uses the following formula:

\[ \text{miles} = \text{km} \times 0.621371 \]

This conversion factor is based on the international definition: 1 kilometer = 0.621371 miles.

Data Source and Methodology

This converter uses the internationally accepted conversion between kilometers and miles:

  • Equivalently: 1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers

Step-by-Step Example

Problem: Convert 10 kilometers to miles.

Step 1: Write down the formula

\[ \text{miles} = \text{km} \times 0.621371 \]

Step 2: Substitute the value

\[ \text{miles} = 10 \times 0.621371 \]

Answer: 10 kilometers = 6.21 miles

Glossary of Terms

Kilometer (km)
A unit of length in the metric system, equal to 1,000 meters. Commonly used worldwide for measuring distances.
Mile (mi)
A unit of length in the imperial system, equal to 1,609.34 meters. Commonly used in the United States and United Kingdom.
Conversion Factor
A number used to convert one set of units to another. For km to miles, the conversion factor is 0.621371.
