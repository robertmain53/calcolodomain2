The Formula Explained

\( \text{Miles} = \text{Kilometers} \times 0.621371 \)

Glossary of Variables

  • Kilometers: Unit of length in the metric system, equal to 1,000 meters.
  • Miles: Unit of length in the imperial system, equal to 1,609.34 meters.

How It Works: A Step-by-Step Example

If you input 10 kilometers, the output in miles will be 10 * 0.621371 = 6.21371 miles.
