How this converter works

The conversion from kilograms to pounds uses the standard international conversion factor. One kilogram equals exactly 2.20462 pounds (based on the international pound definition).

Formula

\( \text{Pounds} = \text{Kilograms} \times 2.20462 \)

Practical Example

What's the difference between kg and lbs?

Kilograms (kg) are the metric unit of mass, used worldwide in science and most countries. Pounds (lbs) are an imperial unit of weight, primarily used in the United States. One pound is lighter than one kilogram.
